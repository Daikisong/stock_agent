# Round-28 Risk Boundary Review

## Green-Possible With Strict Gates
- RARE_METALS_STRATEGIC_MATERIALS: Strategic metals can be Green only when offtake, price floor, production, and FCF are source-backed.
- DATA_CENTER_REIT_INFRASTRUCTURE: Data-center REITs are Green-possible, but FFO/AFFO, occupancy, tenant quality, and funding cost are gates.
