# Round-24 Risk Boundary Review

Round 24 makes several archetypes more precise without loosening Green.

## Green-Possible With Strict Gates
- RAIL_INFRASTRUCTURE: Rail is order-driven like industrial backlog, but margin and delivery risk must be stricter than simple contract headlines.
- CLOUD_AI_SOFTWARE_INFRA: Cloud/SaaS can be Green, but AI wording is only Stage 1 unless recurring revenue, OPM, and FCF improve.
- INSURANCE_UNDERWRITING_CYCLE: Insurance is PBR-ROE-return rerating, not EPS explosion; underwriting, capital, and cyber resilience are gates.
- SECURITY_IDENTITY_DEEPFAKE: Security can be Green, but operational trust is a hard gate because one outage can become 4C.

- 4C cases are thesis-break examples for future calibration, not production labels.
