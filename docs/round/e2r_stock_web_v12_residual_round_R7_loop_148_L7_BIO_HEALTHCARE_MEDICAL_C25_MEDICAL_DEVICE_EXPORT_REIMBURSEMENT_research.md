## 5. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

### 5.1 Positive/control side

Dentium, InBody, and Classys define the positive side of C25. Dentium is the cleanest dental-implant route: export countries were named, China/Vietnam volume was visible, and the same note showed OP margin/earnings strength. InBody shows that a named institutional customer order can matter even when the device category is not a drug-like reimbursement story. Classys shows the highest-quality aesthetic-device template: device sales, consumables, GPM/OPM, and export/global expansion can support Stage2-Actionable and sometimes Yellow, but the post-reprice MAE still requires 4B/profit-lock awareness.

### 5.2 Counterexample side

Vatech, i-SENS, JLK, Lunit, and Ray show the traps. A dental-imaging company can report acceptable revenue and margins but still fail as an entry if revision acceleration and rerating context are absent. A CGM can clear CE MDR and still not rerate if reimbursement, tender access, production ramp, and revenue recognition have not arrived. A medical-AI product can receive non-covered fee status, FDA clearance, or overseas revenue growth, yet still suffer severe MAE when billing volume, installed hospitals, and operating-loss/cash conversion are missing. This is the central residual error: **the current profile can mistake regulatory permission for commercial throughput.**

### 5.3 C25 shadow rule candidate

```text
C25_REIMBURSEMENT_EXPORT_NEEDS_BILLING_VOLUME_REORDER_AND_MARGIN_CONVERSION_GATE

For C25, export/regulatory/reimbursement headlines may unlock Stage2-watch.
Stage2-Actionable to Yellow requires at least two of:
- named overseas customer or distributor with repeat order / installed-base usage
- reimbursement/tender access that has begun converting into billable volume
- consumable/reorder path or recurring service revenue
- OP margin, gross margin, FCF, or loss-ratio improvement
- product-specific capacity or supply readiness

If the event is FDA/CE/fee-code only and billing volume or margin conversion is not visible, route to Stage4B/watch rather than Stage2-Actionable or Yellow.
```
