```text
If a trigger's positive evidence is dominated by price action, headline policy/event language, proxy exposure, or bidder-side control-premium interpretation, require at least one trust-validation field before Stage2-Actionable or Stage3 promotion:
  - customer/order/contract quality,

```

This does not weaken the Stage2 bonus globally. It says the bonus should not be paid when the evidence is a painted door instead of a door that opens.
