## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit

- **Stage2 was excellent** in Devsisters: 90D MFE 618.8 and 180D MFE 790.6 with manageable close-basis drawdown. This validates Stage2-Actionable promotion for game/IP hit evidence.
- **Stage2 was event premium, not structure** in AhnLab: 90D MFE was high, but evidence was political/control premium. This is the key counterexample preventing a naive relative-strength upgrade.
- **Stage2 was good but evidence-caveated** in Douzone: 90D MFE 60.8 and 180D MFE 91.9. Because direct 2024 evidence source is incomplete here, delta magnitude is capped at +1.
- **Stage2 was slow-burn** in NAVER/Webtoon: 90D MFE 9.8 but 1Y MFE 78.4. Platform asset-value rerating needs a longer patience gate.
- **Stage4C mattered** in WEMADE: 4C after 2022-11-24 reduced further downside exposure but was late if only detected after the next-day gap.
