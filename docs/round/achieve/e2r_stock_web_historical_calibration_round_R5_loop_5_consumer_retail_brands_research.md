## 10. 1D Price Path Summaries

### R5L3_C01 삼양식품
Best actionable entry: 2024-03-25 close 206,500. The D+90 high reached 718,000 by 2024-06-19, while the early downside was limited to about -6.3%. Green on 2024-05-20 still worked, but it entered at 502,000 and captured only 43.0% MFE90 instead of 247.7%.

### R5L3_C02 신세계인터내셔날
Best actionable entry: 2018-04-11 close 123,500. The local peak arrived near 224,000 in early July. Stage3-Green on 2018-06-21 was already late and carried inferior MFE/MAE symmetry.

### R5L3_C03 농심
Best actionable entry: 2020-04-24 close 305,000. The case was not a multi-year structural brand rerating, but the 90D path to 401,500 gave a clean cyclical success with shallow initial MAE.

### R5L3_C04 LG생활건강
Baseline Green around 2021-07-08 failed: MFE90 was effectively zero and MAE90 was about -25.3%. 4B should have activated no later than the July 2021 breakdown, and 4C was confirmed by late October/November, but late.

### R5L3_C05 롯데쇼핑
The 2023 retail turnaround Stage2 attempt failed. Entry at 102,000 on 2023-01-18 produced MFE90 0.0 and MAE180 -30.7. Later support breaks should be treated as thesis-break watch rather than delayed Green.
