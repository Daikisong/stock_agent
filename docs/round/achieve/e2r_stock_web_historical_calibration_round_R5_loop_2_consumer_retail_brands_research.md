4. **Good Stage2 evidence combination:** recurring export/channel evidence + relative strength + margin/revision bridge.
5. **Bad Stage2 pattern:** 호텔신라 showed recovery narrative and small RS but lacked margin/revision bridge. That belongs to watch/reject, not Stage2-Actionable.
