## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit
1. POSCO Future M, Enchem and Daejoo show the same mechanism: early evidence was not yet full Green, but it already carried the rerating current. Waiting for Green was like connecting the charger after the battery had already heated up.
2. SKIET is the counterexample. It had early MFE, but the signal was IPO/event premium plus valuation repricing, not durable EPS/FCF evidence. It should not promote structural Stage3.
3. Samsung SDI proves the JV headline guardrail. A real capacity/JV announcement without EV-demand, utilization and margin bridge can become a false-positive score.
4. Therefore the right R3 rule is not “promote all Stage2 battery evidence.” It is “promote early orderbook/capacity/customer-quality evidence only when event-premium, EV-demand, utilization and valuation guardrails pass.”
