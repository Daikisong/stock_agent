### R12L6-C01 한일사료

Stage2 at 2022-02-24 was already an excellent event entry. The 90D MFE was 520.4% with MAE -18.6%. This is not a clean structural EPS case, but it does prove that R12 agriculture shock names can move far before Stage3-Green style confirmation. The correct shadow rule is not “full Green early”; it is “Stage2-Actionable with event-premium risk guard.”

### R12L6-C02 조비

Stage2 at 2022-03-04 had only moderate MFE but much better risk than the later Green-like 2022-05-03 peak entry. Green had MFE90 0.2% and MAE90 -31.6%. This is a direct example of late Green becoming false-positive because it waits until after the event premium is fully crowded.

### R12L6-C03 YBM넷

Stage2 at 2020-02-21 had very high MFE90 of 173.1%, but MAE90 -22.6%. This is acceptable only with small sizing and event guard. The later March entries retained upside but showed deep MAE. For remote education event names, Stage2 can be early, but score should not ignore MAE.

### R12L6-C04 아이스크림에듀

Stage2 at 2020-02-20 had MFE90 57.7% but MAE90 -35.9%. This is a warning case. The engine should not blindly promote any COVID remote-learning name just because early MFE was large. Deep MAE and price-only blowoff need to cap the promotion.

### R12L6-C05 메가스터디교육

This is the cleanest structural R12 case. Stage2 at 2021-02-10 had MFE90 45.3%, MFE180 52.9%, and MAE90 -7.8%. Stage3-Green at 2022-03-16 had only MFE90 6.9% and MAE90 -23.2%. The old gate problem is clear: Green waited until the repricing was already close to exhausted.
