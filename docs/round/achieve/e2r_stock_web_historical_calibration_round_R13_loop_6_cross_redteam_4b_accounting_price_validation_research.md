## 1. Round Scope
R13은 새 종목 발굴이 아니라 **앞선 R1~R12의 점수표를 깨뜨리는 라운드**다. 이번 파일은 Stage2-Actionable 조기 승격이 실제로 필요한지, Stage3-Green이 늦거나 거짓 양성으로 작동한 구간이 있는지, 4B가 가격-only local peak와 비가격 위험 overlay로 분리되어야 하는지, hard 4C가 실제로 drawdown 전에 작동했는지를 검증한다.

이번 R13은 다섯 가지 cross-archetype을 섞었다.

1. HBM/AI 메모리 structural rerating: SK하이닉스

## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit
1. Stage2 trigger에서 MFE가 컸는가?  
   Yes for 000660 and 267260. Their Stage2-Actionable rows produced MFE90 of +52.84% and +200.77% with shallow MAE90 of -4.15% and -6.61%.

2. Stage2 MAE는 감당 가능한 수준이었는가?  
   Structural cases yes. HLB/위메이드/다우데이타 no: the evidence was either binary, token/listing trust, or price-only.

3. Stage2가 Stage3-Green보다 좋은 entry였는가?  
   Yes for the two structural success cases. This is the strongest R13 finding.

4. Stage2가 좋았다면 어떤 evidence 조합 때문인가?  
   Revision/margin bridge/relative strength/customer-quality or backlog visibility. It was not “price up” alone.

5. Stage2가 나빴다면 왜 나빴는가?  
   HLB had unresolved binary approval risk. 위메이드는 listing/accounting-trust risk. 다우데이타는 price-only leverage unwind, not an EPS rerating.

## 24. Score-Price Alignment Verdict
R13 does **not** say “always buy Stage2.” It says:

- Stage2 becomes actionable only when there is a real mechanism: revision + margin/backlog/customer-quality + relative strength.
- Stage3-Green should remain a high-confidence tier, but it must report lateness ratio and upside already consumed.
- Price-only RS must stay in watch/reject unless evidence arrives.
- Binary approval, listing, CFD/leverage, accounting-trust and legal/regulatory risks must be risk overlays before they become hard 4C.
- 4B must split local price peak and full-window non-price risk; otherwise it exits structural leaders too early and still misses fraud/liquidity events.
