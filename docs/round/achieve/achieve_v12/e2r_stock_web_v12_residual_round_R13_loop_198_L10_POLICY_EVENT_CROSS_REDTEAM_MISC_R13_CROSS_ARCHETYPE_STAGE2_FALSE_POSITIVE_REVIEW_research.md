## 6. Stage2 false-positive taxonomy

The repeated error is not that Stage2 is early. Stage2 is supposed to be early. The error is **single-variable elevation**: one headline is mistaken for a conversion bridge.

| taxonomy | false-positive mechanism | required second bridge before Actionable / Yellow | representative rows |
|---|---|---|---|
| long_lead_resource_capacity_without_cashflow | strategic capacity or sovereignty language is treated like near-term earnings | shipment, offtake, realized margin, working-capital/cash conversion | 005490 |
| preliminary_buyer_or_governance_noise_without_cashflow | resource-policy or buyer-talk language is mixed with control-premium/governance price action | signed offtake, realized volume, margin bridge, governance-noise exclusion | 010130 |
| supply_contract_theme_without_near_term_conversion | direct supply evidence exists but entry is late and no near-term earnings conversion is visible | delivery schedule, revenue recognition, margin/cashflow conversion | 006260 |
| commodity_profile_earnings_swing_without_order_bridge | product/profile exposure or earnings swing is mistaken for company-specific order conversion | issuer-level order/backlog, margin bridge, customer pull | 024840, 000500 |
| record_spread_profit_after_peak_without_duration | peak-cycle profit is treated as persistent spread power | forward spread duration, cost pass-through, shipment/order route | 005010, 298020 |
| direct_bridge_positive_control | a real order/offtake/backlog bridge should preserve Stage2-Actionable | keep Stage2, but block Green until cashflow/MAE risk clears | 229640, 103590, 306200, 373220, 003670 |
