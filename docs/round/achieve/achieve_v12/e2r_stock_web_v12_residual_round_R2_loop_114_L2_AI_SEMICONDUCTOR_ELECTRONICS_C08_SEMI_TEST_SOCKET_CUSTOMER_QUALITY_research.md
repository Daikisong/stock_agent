## 3. Residual Mechanism

C08 is a narrow semicap archetype. The mechanism is not “semiconductor quality” in the abstract. It is more like a small valve in a pressure system: customer qualification opens the valve, repeat socket/test consumption keeps flow going, and OPM/FCF proves the pressure reached the company’s income statement. If the row is really broad equipment-cycle beta, valuation blowoff, or material spread, the C08 label becomes a mask.

The profile’s residual error is concentrated in three places:

1. **Pure C08 positives**: test handler, repeat socket, or service/cleaning revenue with customer reorder and margin bridge.
2. **Boundary positives**: metrology, laser, or process-equipment names where quality is real but C07/C09/C10 may be the dominant mechanism.
3. **False positives**: component/material/fabless/electronics labels where the price path looks thematic but the C08 bridge is missing.
