## 3. Score-Return Alignment Stress Test

C24 is shaped like a loaded spring: when a true endpoint/data event changes probability, the move can be violent and structurally meaningful. But when the same words are pasted onto an already-commercial product, a medical device export label, a holding-company bio theme, or a post-peak pipeline bounce, the spring is not attached to the mechanism. It snaps, but it does not move the machine.

| bucket | rule interpretation | observed row pattern | corrected treatment |
|---|---|---|---|
| validated binary/data event | endpoint quality or licensing/data de-risking is fresh and asset-specific | low-to-medium MAE with strong 90/180D MFE | Stage3 may be allowed, still with MAE guard |
| commercialization/approved drug | sales, approval, prescription, royalty or reimbursement dominates | C23 rows show commercial bridge, not trial-data event | reroute to C23 |
| medical device/export/reimbursement | installed base, consumables, device service revenue dominates | C25 rows can have MFE but mechanism is not C24 | reroute to C25 |
| price-only bio spike | no endpoint quality, no clean cash path, high MAE | local 4B/high-MAE rows | local 4B only or Stage2 cap |
