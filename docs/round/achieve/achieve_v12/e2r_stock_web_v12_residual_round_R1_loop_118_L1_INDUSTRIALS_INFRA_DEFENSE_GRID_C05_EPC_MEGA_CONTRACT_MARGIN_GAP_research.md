## 4. Mechanism summary

C05 should not treat an EPC/construction contract headline, a PF rescue headline, or a liquidity repair headline as a completed Stage3 rerating. The bridge must be visible in the following order: contract or liquidity event → revenue recognition → realized margin → working-capital normalization → cash collection / FCF. When that bridge is missing, even apparently good MFE paths can conceal a deep post-peak drawdown, so the safer routing is Stage2-Watch or local Stage4B overlay rather than Stage3-Green.

The five usable rows split into three positive-with-guardrail rows and two counterexamples. The average 180D MFE is `25.4989` while the average 180D MAE is `-21.5902`; this means the archetype can still produce upside, but it must demand cash and margin evidence before promotion.
