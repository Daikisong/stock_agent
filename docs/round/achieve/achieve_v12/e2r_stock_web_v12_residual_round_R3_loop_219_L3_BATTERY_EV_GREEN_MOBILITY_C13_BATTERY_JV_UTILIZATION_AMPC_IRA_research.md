## Stage Evidence Separation

### Positive / guardrail-positive rows

1. `SOLUS_CANADA_VES_PLANT_2024`: Canada battery-copper-foil construction was a specific localized capacity route. It was not pure policy air; it had plant, geography, and capacity. The 90D/180D path supports Stage2-Actionable to Yellow, but still requires utilization/cash confirmation before Green.
2. `LEM_Q2_2024_NA_SALES`: North-American sales expansion was real enough to capture an early move, but the later drawdown proves that volume bridge and margin/cash bridge are not the same thing.
3. `SOLUS_1Q25_CUSTOMER_SALES_DROP`: a customer-sales decline should be a 4B watch / route-stress signal, not automatic 4C, unless the customer or plant route dies.

### Counterexample rows

1. `LEM_STARPLUS_2024_EXCLUSIVE_SUPPLY`: a direct customer route after local rerating gave almost no 180D upside and very large downside. The contract is a pipe, but the pipe was priced before water moved through it.
2. `SOLUS_3Q24_RESULT_AFTER_RERATING`: result/IR narrative after the earlier capacity rerating had stale evidence and large MAE.
3. `DAEJOO_2024_IR_SILICON_ANODE_ROUTE`: product/customer expansion language without direct calloff/utilization was not enough.
4. `SKC_NEXILIS_NORTHVOLT_CONTRACT`: a large direct contract still suffered high 180D MAE because utilization and margin conversion lagged the contract headline.
5. `LEM_2024_RESULTS_UTILIZATION_LOSS`: negative utilization evidence after reset was not thesis death, but it showed why Green based on prior North America sales would have been too generous.
