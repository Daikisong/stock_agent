## 5. Case notes

### CASE01 — i-SENS / 099190 — first CGM launch was too early

CareSens Air had a real device and reimbursement bridge: Kakao Healthcare described MFDS approval, insurance listing, domestic launch, and a distribution/integrated service agreement. But stock-web shows that the 2023-09-20 entry had only +11.33% 180D MFE and -35.58% 180D MAE. C25 should not convert first-launch/reimbursement vocabulary directly into Stage2-Actionable unless revenue ramp is visible.

### CASE02 — i-SENS / 099190 — post-reset CGM product improvement worked better

The 2025 upgrade removed calibration, shortened warm-up time, and followed launches in 14 countries during 2024. The reset entry on 2025-03-04 produced +31.18% 180D MFE with -17.81% MAE. This is not a generic device-launch rule; it argues for a C25 post-reset product-improvement + global-expansion bridge.

### CASE03 — Curexo / 060280 — PMDA approval plus named distributor was real but needed staged entry

Curexo's PMDA approval and Kyocera distribution rights gave a hard regulatory and channel bridge. The 2025-03-20 entry produced +59.72% 180D MFE but also -26.28% 90D MAE. This supports C25 Stage2-Actionable, but only with high-MAE staged-entry guard.

### CASE04 — Mirae Company / 049950 — Revo-i Paraguay export was a valid event-cap positive

Mirae Company's Revo-i export contract to Paraguay created a clean medical-robot export bridge. The stock-web path produced +42.78% 30D/90D/180D MFE, but the peak occurred quickly and drawdown after peak reached -45.94%. C25 should treat signed export as positive, while adding local 4B event-cap exit guard.

### CASE05 — Boditech Med / 206640 — installed-base POCT route was useful before the late headline

Boditech's Q1 evidence supported the POCT installed-base/consumable route. The 2024-05-13 entry produced +27.34% 90D/180D MFE with tolerable -9.26% 90D MAE. This is the cleanest C25 diagnostic-device positive in this loop.

### CASE06 — Boditech Med / 206640 — Q2 result headline was late

The Q2 headline still looked good, but entry timing was worse. The 2024-08-09 trigger produced only +8.00% MFE across 30D/90D/180D and -31.50% 90D/180D MAE. C25 must distinguish early installed-base leading indicators from late result headlines.

### CASE07 — NanoEntek / 039860 — FDA-approved IVD portfolio needed revenue conversion guard

NanoEntek's FREND portfolio had FDA approvals and global sales markets, but the price path was volatile: +43.69% 180D MFE with -31.08% 180D MAE and a -48.85% post-peak drawdown. This is a high-MAE success, not a clean Green unlock.

### CASE08 — Seegene / 096530 — molecular diagnostics platform had value, but not automatic Green

Seegene's Microsoft collaboration for SG OneSystem created a plausible MDx platform/technology-sharing bridge. The 2024-01-23 entry produced +42.94% 180D MFE but also -22.47% 180D MAE. C25 should credit platform diagnostics only when value capture is visible, and keep 4B/high-MAE guard.

### CASE09 — SD Biosensor / 137310 — global IVD direct-sales plan could not beat post-Covid decay

SD Biosensor's Meridian, direct-sales expansion, STANDARD M10, and CGMS plan sounded like a C25 global diagnostics rerating. The stock-web path was the opposite: only +2.48% MFE and -67.48% 180D MAE. This is the key counterexample: post-Covid demand decay can dominate impressive diagnostic-platform plans.

## 6. Sector-specific rule candidate

```text
sector_specific_rule_candidate = L7_C25_DEVICE_EXPORT_REIMBURSEMENT_REVENUE_RAMP_AND_ENTRY_TIMING_GATE_V2
canonical_archetype_rule_candidate = C25_REIMBURSEMENT_EXPORT_INSTALLED_BASE_AND_REVENUE_RAMP_GATE_V2
```

C25 should not treat `approval`, `device launch`, `insurance listing`, `distribution agreement`, or `global diagnostics plan` as sufficient by themselves. The promotion bridge should require at least one commercial path and one conversion path.

```text
commercial_path_candidates:
- reimbursement or billing route with usage ramp
- named distributor or export contract
- installed diagnostic instrument base leading to consumables
- regulatory approval plus launch/distribution right
- molecular diagnostics platform partnership with commercial value capture

conversion_path_candidates:
- revenue ramp or consumable sales evidence
- margin or operating-profit bridge
- installed-base utilization
- market-share or recurring test volume
- low-MAE or reset-after-drawdown timing confirmation
```
