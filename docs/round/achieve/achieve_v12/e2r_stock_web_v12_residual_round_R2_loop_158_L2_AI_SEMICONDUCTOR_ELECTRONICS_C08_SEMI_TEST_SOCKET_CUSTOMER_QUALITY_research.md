## 5. Case-level interpretation

### C08-L158-001 — 리노공업, early socket-quality/margin bridge

The March 2024 Leeno report gave a clean C08 bridge: high-margin product mix, advanced packaging/high-bandwidth socket commercialization, and 2024 sales/OP forecast. The entry on the next tradable day captured strong upside, but the later drawdown means the positive rule should be paired with a local 4B exit guard.

### C08-L158-002 / 003 — 리노공업, same thesis after the oxygen is already burning

The same business quality did not protect later entries. The mechanism is important: a socket-quality company can stay fundamentally good while the entry becomes poor because valuation and relative strength already pulled forward the signal. This is exactly where C08 needs a `good thesis but late entry` branch.

### C08-L158-004 — ISC, VIP customer and AI socket route

The February 2024 Asiae piece supplied the early customer-quality bridge: AI-related sales targets, Samsung/SK Hynix among VIP customers, and North American fabless exposure. This is the type of C08 evidence that deserves Stage2-Actionable before a confirmed Green label.

### C08-L158-005 / 006 — ISC, report-confirmed thesis but late/high-MAE entry

The March 29 report gave a strong HBM/AI GPU socket narrative, 2024F sales/OP forecast, and a rubber-socket product bridge. However, the report's own current-price context was already near the local peak. The price path then produced only small MFE with very large MAE. This is not a thesis failure; it is a C08 timing/valuation failure.

## 7. Shadow rule candidate

```text
rule_id = C08_SOCKET_CUSTOMER_QUALITY_REVENUE_BRIDGE_AND_EXIT_GATE_V1
rule_scope = canonical_archetype_specific
production_scoring_changed = false

```

Candidate rule:

C08 should receive Stage2-Actionable only when at least one **customer-quality bridge** and one **conversion bridge** are both present.

Customer-quality bridge examples:

- VIP customer base with named semiconductor customers.
- AI/HBM/GPU socket exposure tied to actual product family, not just theme words.
- Advanced-package/high-bandwidth socket commercialization route.

Conversion bridge examples:

- Sales/OP forecast, margin bridge, socket ASP/product-mix improvement.
- Near-term revenue recognition or product shipment path.
- Evidence that R&D/qualification demand is becoming recurring or production-linked.

Guard:

If the entry occurs after a rapid local rerating and the report/evidence already names a high current price or high valuation, demote from Stage2-Actionable/Yellow to `Stage4B-Watch` unless a fresh order/revenue bridge appears. In lived-market terms: the socket thesis can be a good engine, but the belt may already be running too hot.
