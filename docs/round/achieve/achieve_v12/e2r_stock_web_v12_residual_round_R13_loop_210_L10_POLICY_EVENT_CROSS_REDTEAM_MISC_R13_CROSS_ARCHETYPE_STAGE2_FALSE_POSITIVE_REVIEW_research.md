## 6. Residual Error Interpretation

The same failure mode appears in three different costumes.

In **C01**, component backlog can be real, but Stage2-Actionable is fragile when the market only sees component exposure or event-cap rerating before revenue/margin timing is proven. HD현대마린솔루션 and 대양전기공업 are useful guardrail rows because the evidence looked industrially relevant, yet the forward path required 4B/high-MAE discipline.

In **C30**, PF and construction repair headlines behave like rescue ropes. Some are attached to a real beam, such as HJ중공업 or HS화성, while others are attached to loose scaffolding: generic PF risk relief, weak earnings, or post-shock loss headlines. The rule must avoid both mistakes: promoting broad policy/PF relief without company repair, and hard-4C blocking a reset-price recovery.

In **C18**, export growth is not automatically reorder quality. 삼양식품 and the later 농심 reset have a clearer money road. CJ제일제당 and 매일유업 show the opposite: global-food or single-channel export language without enough listed-entity margin conversion can produce low MFE and deep MAE.
