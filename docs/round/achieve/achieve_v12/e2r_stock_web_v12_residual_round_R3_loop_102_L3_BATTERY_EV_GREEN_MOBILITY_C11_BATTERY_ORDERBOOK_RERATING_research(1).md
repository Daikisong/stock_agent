## 3. Hypothesis

C11 is useful only when orderbook evidence is translated into economics. A contract, capacity ramp, or equipment demand label can open Stage2, but it should not move to Yellow or Green unless at least one bridge appears:

1. revenue conversion from orderbook,
2. margin/FCF visibility,
3. confirmed revision,
4. customer quality that is more than a generic supply-chain proxy,
5. no local overheat or equipment-cycle beta fade.

The new residual question here is whether **battery supplier/equipment proxies** should be compressed into C11 or separately guarded. The price path says they can remain inside C11, but they need a harsher conversion bridge than cathode/cell direct orderbook cases.
