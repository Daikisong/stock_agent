## 7. Backtest method
Entry uses the first tradable Stock-Web row on or after the trigger date. `entry_price` is close. `MFE_ND_pct` is `(max high in first N trading rows / entry close - 1) * 100`; `MAE_ND_pct` is `(min low in first N trading rows / entry close - 1) * 100`. The 30/90/180 windows are trading-day windows, not calendar-day windows.
