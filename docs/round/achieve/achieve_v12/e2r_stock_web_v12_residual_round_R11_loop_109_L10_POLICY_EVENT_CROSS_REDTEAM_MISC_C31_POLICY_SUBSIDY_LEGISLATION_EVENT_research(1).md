## 3. Residual Mechanism

C31 is the policy switchboard. It does not own the cash flow by itself. The policy impulse is like a traffic light: it changes which road opens, but the actual vehicle still has to travel through a sector-specific mechanism. For banks, that road is capital return and ROE. For batteries, it is AMPC/utilization/call-off. For grid and defense, it is backlog and margin conversion. For healthcare, it is reimbursement, approval, or device/service revenue. For governance, it is minority cash path and tender cap.

The profile’s residual error is over-scoring policy vocabulary before the cash bridge is known. This loop therefore strengthens C31 as an initial classifier and reroute gate, not as a standalone Green engine.
