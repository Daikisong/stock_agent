## 7. Rule Candidate

```text
sector_specific_rule_candidate: L7_MEDICAL_DEVICE_COMMERCIALIZATION_SECOND_BRIDGE_GATE
canonical_archetype_rule_candidate: C25_REIMBURSEMENT_PROCEDURE_VOLUME_SECOND_BRIDGE_GATE
rule_scope: canonical_archetype_specific
```

Core residual rule:

- FDA / CE / MFDS approval, innovative-device designation, reimbursement expectation, export market entry, or distributor announcement alone does not create Stage3-Yellow or Stage3-Green in C25.
- Stage2-Actionable requires at least one direct second bridge: actual reimbursement listing, procedure volume, installed-base growth, consumables/reorder revenue, partner distribution with launch timing, realized revenue, or margin conversion.
- A named distributor plus commercial launch route can preserve Stage2-Actionable, as in the Classys positive-control row.
- High MAE after approval-only or distribution-only evidence blocks Yellow/Green first; it does not automatically erase Stage2 if the regulatory option remains alive.
- Weak quarter / inventory write-down / digital-dentistry miss routes first to Stage4B/watch. Hard Stage4C requires regulatory rejection, reimbursement failure, customer/procedure collapse, accounting-trust break, or weak offset quality.
