## 6. Residual interpretation

This loop adds `8` new independent cases, `3` counterexamples, and `7` residual errors for `L1_INDUSTRIALS_INFRA_DEFENSE_GRID/C05_EPC_MEGA_CONTRACT_MARGIN_GAP`.

The recurring pattern is not that EPC or engineering contracts fail. It is that C05 has two different pipes that look similar on the headline tape. One pipe is a direct customer/order route that can move water into margin and cash. The other is a source-profile or broad sector route where the pipe exists but the water pressure never reaches the listed issuer. The current profile can still over-promote the second pipe when it treats engineering capability, supplier profile, or report text as equivalent to realized margin/cash conversion.

### Sector-specific rule candidate

`L1 EPC/engineering/supplier rows should split route/order existence, customer finality, margin bridge, working-capital/cash conversion, and price phase. Contract evidence is the pipe; margin and cash are the water.`

### Canonical archetype rule candidate

`C05 should add an engineering/design/plant/ship-equipment order-to-margin-cash guard: route evidence -> direct customer/order finality -> margin/cost proof -> working-capital/cash conversion -> phase sanity. Route-only or source-profile rows after rerating are 4B-watch; high-MAE with a surviving route is not hard 4C.`
