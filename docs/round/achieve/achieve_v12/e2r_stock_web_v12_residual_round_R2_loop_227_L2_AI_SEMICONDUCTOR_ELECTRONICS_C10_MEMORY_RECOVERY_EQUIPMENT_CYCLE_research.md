## 6. Residual interpretation

C10 should not treat every HBM/CXL/memory-cycle sentence as the same kind of bridge. A theme headline is weather. Product route is a pipe. Customer qualification, booked order, shipment, utilization, and margin/cash conversion are the water moving through it. This loop isolates three residual failure modes:

1. **Generic proxy overpromotion** — CXL/socket/probe-card/material narratives without issuer-level order conversion produced deep MAE rows.
2. **Route after reset success** — YC Chem, BCnC, Duksan Hi-Metal, and Pemtron show that the same proxy-to-product evidence becomes usable when the entry is after price reset and the route is more issuer-specific.
3. **Direct evidence after rerating cap** — IMT shows that even direct delivery evidence can require 4B-watch if the price already paid for the route before entry.
