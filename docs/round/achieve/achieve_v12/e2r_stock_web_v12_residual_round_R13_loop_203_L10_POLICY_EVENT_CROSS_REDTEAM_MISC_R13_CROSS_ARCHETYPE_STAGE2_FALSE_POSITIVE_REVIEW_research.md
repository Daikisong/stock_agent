## 1. Scope
This is not a new sector-specific Cxx study. It is an R13 cross-archetype checkpoint after loops 195~202. The goal is to test whether the current calibrated profile still lets Stage2 or Stage2-Actionable fire on **bridge words** — policy, product category, demo, theme, contract size, proxy exposure, or generic customer vocabulary — before the evidence becomes an issuer-level money road.

## 5. Cross-Archetype Finding
The recurring error is not sector-specific. It appears in steel/metal spread proxies, EPC mega-contract headlines, game/IP launch events, cloud/security product categories, policy/subsidy events, and nuclear supplier proxies. The common mechanism is:

```text
bridge word appears
  -> current profile grants too much visibility / evidence confidence
  -> entry occurs before issuer-level money road is visible
  -> MFE90 remains weak or MAE90 becomes deep
  -> local 4B or Stage2 false positive appears
```

The four control positives show the other side of the rule. Hanwha Aerospace had a sovereign Romania K9/K10 execution contract, SAMG had IP retained-economics/MD monetization, Gabia had cloud/IDC/security operating conversion, and Daewoo E&C had a framework-to-contract commercial bridge. These should not be overblocked.
