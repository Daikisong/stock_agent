## 5. Case interpretation

### 5.1 036930 주성엔지니어링 — real ALD equipment, but not Green without conversion

The as-of signal is a real equipment signal, not a material proxy. JUSUNG had semiconductor deposition equipment identity and a 2024 expansion story from memory into non-memory. The stock-web path had positive 30D/90D MFE, so a pure keyword block would be too strict. But 180D MAE reached -29.21% and post-peak drawdown reached -46.80%, so the correct C09 treatment is Stage2-Actionable plus local 4B after fast MFE, not clean Stage3-Green.

### 5.2 064760 티씨케이 — process-critical component exception

TCK is not a tool supplier, but SiC focus rings are process-critical components for dry etch. The low-MAE, high-MFE path says C09 should not be limited only to classic equipment tools. A narrow `tool-only` gate misses bottleneck process-component rerating. The exception should require utilization/demand bridge, not just a component label.

### 5.3 014680 한솔케미칼 and 357780 솔브레인 — material proxy block

Both names had semiconductor-material narratives. Those narratives can be valid in C10/C15/C17 or material-specific research, but they should not automatically become C09 advanced-equipment positives. The price paths show modest MFE and deep MAE. This strengthens the rule that materials-only exposure needs a separate canonical route unless the row proves tool/process-component bottleneck conversion.

### 5.4 183300 코미코 — result confirmation after price reprice

Komico had strong 1Q24 review evidence around cleaning/coating demand, but the trigger date behaved like a late confirmation row: MFE was only 6.15% while MAE_180D was -65.97%. The lesson is not that cleaning/coating is irrelevant. The lesson is that accounting confirmation after the stock has already moved must be capped as local 4B unless it is accompanied by fresh order/customer/revenue expansion.

### 5.5 089970 브이엠 — real tool identity can still be a bad entry

VM is a genuine etch-equipment route. However the June 2024 report date was too close to crowding: MFE capped at 18.16% while MAE_180D reached -68.98%. C09 needs a freshness check. Real tool identity is necessary, but not sufficient after price has already pulled forward the cycle.

### 5.6 083450 GST — narrative-only proxy trap

GST shows why proxy repair matters. Scrubber/chiller and AI immersion-cooling language can look like a C09-equipment story, but without a direct order/revenue bridge it should be Stage2-watch or local 4B at most. The stock-web raw/unadjusted window also has corporate-action contamination, so it is not representative for aggregate scoring.

## 6. Proposed shadow rule

```text
C09_ADVANCED_EQUIPMENT_REQUIRES_PROCESS_ROLE_ORDER_OR_REVENUE_CONVERSION_WITH_MATERIAL_PROXY_BLOCK_AND_POST_REPRICE_4B_CAP
```

Rule mechanics:

1. Stage2-watch is allowed for real advanced equipment, process equipment, and process-critical component identity.
2. Stage2-Actionable requires at least one fresh non-price bridge: named customer, PO/order, backlog, qualification, shipment, utilization-driven demand, or revenue/margin conversion.
3. Materials-only exposure, chemical/pre-cursor exposure, generic AI cooling, and late accounting confirmation are not C09 positives by themselves.
4. If 30D/90D MFE has already occurred and the next evidence is not a new PO/order/conversion bridge, cap the row at local Stage4B.
5. Corporate-action-contaminated raw/unadjusted windows remain narrative-only even if the story is useful.
