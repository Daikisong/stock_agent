## 5. Holdout design

The holdout pack deliberately mixes two categories:

1. **Structural high-MAE successes.** HD현대일렉트릭 and LS ELECTRIC experienced deep interim drawdowns, but the original evidence bridge was direct, dated, and non-price: IR/earnings, product mapping, grid equipment exposure, backlog/customer/order evidence. These should not be cut solely by MAE.
2. **High-MAE false positives or protection cases.** 대원전선, 싸이맥스, 유니테스트, SK이노베이션, 금호건설, 대동기어, 유니온머티리얼 had missing or contradicted symbol-level bridges. Their MAE is not just volatility; it is the price path revealing that the original thesis lacked a durable rail.

## 6. Case notes

### 6.1 HD현대일렉트릭 / 267260 — structural winner with high interim MAE

The 2025-01-20 IR/earnings trigger had direct grid-equipment exposure and earnings/order bridge. The entry still saw MAE90 around -31.30%, but MFE180 reached +81.04%. R13 should treat this as **high-MAE success preserved by evidence quality**, not automatic 4C.

### 6.2 LS ELECTRIC / 010120 — structural winner with drawdown-aware Green delay

The 4Q 2024 IR trigger had U.S. HVTR/SWGR demand and backlog/customer expansion evidence. MAE90 reached -29.08%, while MFE180 reached +61.35%. The rule should preserve Stage2/YELLOW but delay Green until drawdown-aware confirmation.

### 6.3 대원전선 / 006340 — price-only grid theme blowoff

The trigger was proxy/theme-led: U.S. transformer expectation and AI/data-center power narrative without symbol-level order/backlog bridge. MFE180 was only +9.77%, while MAE180 reached -49.31%. R13 should route this toward local 4B / non-actionable watch.

### 6.4 싸이맥스 / 160980 — memory CapEx headline without order conversion

Customer CapEx headlines are not enough for C10. The stock had MFE180 +7.22% and MAE180 -66.55%, so this is a high-MAE false positive if product mapping is treated as order/revenue conversion.

### 6.5 유니테스트 / 086390 — long-term investment news without near-term tester conversion

Long-term AI/chip investment language did not provide near-term tester order bridge. MFE180 was +4.56%, MAE180 -48.38%. This is a 4C-style protection case.

### 6.6 SK이노베이션 / 096770 — AMPC not enough when loss bridge dominates

AMPC was real, but consolidated/segment loss and non-battery exposure dominated. MFE180 was +5.09%, MAE180 -52.50%. R13 should require profitability/utilization bridge before Stage2-Actionable survives.

### 6.7 금호건설 / 002990 — contract headline without cost-to-complete bridge

The public contract headline had a customer and amount, but no margin, cost-to-complete, or working-capital cleanup bridge. MFE180 was +4.73%, MAE180 -41.12%. The R13 lesson is that headline contracts can be traps when financial quality is already thin.

### 6.8 대동기어 / 008830 — financial quality veto

The row is already Stage4C in the C01 source loop. R13 keeps it as a hard protection example: backlog/story language cannot override financial-quality veto and subsequent high-MAE path.

### 6.9 유니온머티리얼 / 047400 — policy proxy without symbol-level resource bridge

The MOFCOM gallium/germanium event was a real policy shock, but the symbol-level resource/offtake/cash bridge was missing. MFE180 was +14.00%, MAE180 -38.50%. R13 should treat this as a policy-proxy 4C/4B risk, not C16 Stage2 evidence.

## 9. 4B local vs full-window proximity

R13 reinforces the split between local 4B and full 4B. 대원전선, 싸이맥스, 유니테스트, 금호건설, and 유니온머티리얼 show price paths where peak came early or upside was capped while drawdown deepened. Those are not “wait for full-window peak” situations; they require local watch/4B when non-price bridge is weak.

For HD현대일렉트릭 and LS ELECTRIC, the same drawdown depth should not automatically demote the case because direct evidence remained intact. The engine needs a drawdown-aware confirmation delay, not a kill switch.
