## 13. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

The existing global axes mostly help, but the residual leak is cross-sector. C02 power proxy, C07/C09 HBM-adjacent equipment, C11 battery equipment orderbook, C25 reimbursement, C28 software recurring-like revenue, and C32 control premium all fail in the same way: the evidence has a plausible word, but not enough delivery, retention, margin, or execution bridge to survive high-MAE entry.

## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison

No new Stage3-Green unlock is proposed. The loop instead weakens false Stage2-Actionable/Yellow attempts when the signal family has already produced MAE90 <= -40% without fresh non-price bridge.

## 15. 4B Local vs Full-window Timing Audit

High-MAE survivor rows should be routed to local 4B/profit-lock or staged-entry rules. They should not automatically become hard 4C unless a non-price thesis break appears.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit

The HDC Hyundai Development trust-break row is the control: quality/safety/trust failure plus deep MAE validates hard 4C. The other rows are mostly not hard 4C; they are weak-bridge, scope-proxy, or entry-timing failures.
