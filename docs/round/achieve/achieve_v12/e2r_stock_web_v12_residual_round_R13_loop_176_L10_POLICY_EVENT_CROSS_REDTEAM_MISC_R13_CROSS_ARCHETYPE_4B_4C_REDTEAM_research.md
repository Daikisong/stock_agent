## 3. R13 4B/4C Thesis

4B and 4C are not the same alarm bell.

- **Stage4B** is a hot brake: price or event asymmetry has turned poor, but the core thesis may still be alive. Examples: tender cap, approval spike, late quality entry, single-brand event cap, price-only HBM blowoff.
- **Stage4C** is a broken axle: the non-price thesis is actually broken. Examples: vote failure, call-off/order cut, loss conversion, qualification failure, regulatory/trust break.
- **False 4C** happens when the word looks scary but the mechanism is not broken: legal delay while project stays alive, segment loss with customer offset, or a project-loss shock after valuation has already reset.

## 6. Residual Findings

### 6.1 Full 4B should not require permanent thesis break

SM Entertainment, DIT, SK Bioscience, Lotte Wellfood, and Leeno Industrial show the same shape: price or event premium was already near exhaustion, while the underlying evidence had not yet become a hard 4C. If the engine waits for full thesis break before placing a 4B watch, it misses the useful exit/position-size signal.

### 6.2 Hard 4C needs a confirmation gate

Hanmi Science and WCP confirm that vote failure, call-off/order cut, and loss-conversion evidence can justify hard 4C. The mechanism is no longer a shadow on the wall; the axle is cracked.

### 6.3 Hard 4C can be too early

Solus Advanced Materials, GS E&C, and Doosan Enerbility are the counterweights. EV slowdown, project-loss shock, and legal-delay language were real, but each had a remaining offset: customer-requested supply expansion, valuation reset after the shock, or a project still alive after appeal rejection. These rows argue for `Stage4C-Watch` or `4B/audit` first, not immediate irreversible 4C.

Rule candidate:

```text
IF event_cap OR late_entry_after_rerating OR approval_spike_without_demand_bridge OR single-brand_event_without_company_margin_bridge OR price_only_blowoff
THEN mark Stage4B-Watch even if hard 4C is not confirmed.

IF vote_failure OR contract_cancelled OR order_cut/call_off OR loss_conversion_with_target_cut OR qualification_failure OR irreversible regulatory/trust break
THEN allow hard Stage4C.

IF scary headline exists BUT customer/project/offset remains alive OR price has already reset and 180D MAE is mild while MFE is positive
THEN use false-break audit / Stage4C-Watch, not hard Stage4C.
```
