## 3. Mechanism Diagnosis

Stage2 is a smoke alarm. It is allowed to ring early. The bug appears when the smoke alarm is mistaken for the fire engine: source-proxy evidence, sector-label momentum, and early MFE are not enough for Stage3-Green or full 4B. Across semicap, materials, consumer, finance, policy, and governance rows, the same residual appears:

- `good_stage2`: Stage2 is useful and should remain early, but promotion waits for URL/profile/shard repair.
- `mixed_stage2`: Stage2 is plausible but mechanism boundary or high MAE requires Yellow/Local-4B cap.
- `bad_stage2`: Stage2 itself may be a watch signal, but any promotion would be a false positive.
