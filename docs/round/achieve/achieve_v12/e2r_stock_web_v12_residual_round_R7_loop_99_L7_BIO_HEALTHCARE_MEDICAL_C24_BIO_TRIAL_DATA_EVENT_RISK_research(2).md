## 4. Residual interpretation

C24 is not one disease; it is a ward with several patients who all wear the same “clinical event” wristband. A Phase 3 endpoint success, an affiliate CRL readthrough, a local rebound after damaged data, and a trial-label spike look similar in the headline tape but behave differently in the 1D price path.

The residual rule is therefore a split gate. For Yellow/Green, require verified endpoint, partner/license, regulatory, or commercialization bridge. For 4C, require direct thesis exposure and confirmed thesis break. If the event is only affiliate/proxy readthrough or a non-direct CRL shock, the safer route is Local4B/4C-watch, not automatic hard 4C.
