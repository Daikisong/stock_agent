## 3. Residual Mechanism

C26 is the operating-leverage archetype for ad and platform revenue. The signal is not just “advertising recovered.” The gearbox is budget retention, ARPU or ad-load quality, client reorder, take-rate, segment margin, and OPM revision. If those gears do not engage, the row is just a keyword pop.

The main residual errors are:

- small adtech names getting promoted after AI/ad-budget spikes without retention;
- large platforms getting credit without segment margin proof;
- content/IP rows from C27 and software/security rows from C28 leaking into C26;
- agency budget-cycle rows treated like structural platform operating leverage.
