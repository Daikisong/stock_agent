## Residual findings

1. **Verified channel bridge beats label-only rerating.** 257720, 018290, 003230, and 161890 show that C20 can work when export/distribution velocity is tied to reorder, capacity, or margin visibility. The price path reward was large enough to justify Stage2-Actionable or Stage3-Yellow, but source URL repair is still required before promotion.
2. **Small-cap K-beauty and K-food labels can become local 4B before a full thesis break.** 214420 and 005180 had high local MFE but also deep post-peak drawdowns. C20 should not route those as clean Green without a verified sell-through and margin bridge.
3. **Large-cap food distribution needs acceleration evidence.** 271560 produced a lower and slower MFE path than the high-beta beauty/food exporters. The label alone is not enough for Yellow.
4. **ODM positives need timing guardrails.** 192820 had a legitimate ODM channel story but the May 2024 trigger sat close enough to a local peak that C20 should maintain a 4B watch unless the order/margin bridge is refreshed after the first spike.
