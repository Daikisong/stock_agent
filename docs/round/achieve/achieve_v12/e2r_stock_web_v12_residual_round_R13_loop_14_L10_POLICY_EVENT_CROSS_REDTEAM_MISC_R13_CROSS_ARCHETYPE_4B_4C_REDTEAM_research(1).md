## 3. Mechanism Diagnosis

4B is a cliff edge. Local 4B is the ledge you can stand on for a moment; full 4B is the bridge that holds weight across the full window. The model’s residual error is treating a ledge like a bridge.

The guardrail is simple:

- **Local-4B-Watch**: price proximity or early MFE exists, but non-price bridge is not freshly verified.
- **Full 4B candidate after reverify**: MFE and MAE are favorable enough, and a real cash/order/margin/endpoint bridge plausibly exists.
- **Hard 4C / 4C Watch**: deep MAE or thesis break overwhelms the early MFE.
- **Narrative-only blocked**: corporate-action, workout, or discontinuity means the row cannot calibrate until refetched.
