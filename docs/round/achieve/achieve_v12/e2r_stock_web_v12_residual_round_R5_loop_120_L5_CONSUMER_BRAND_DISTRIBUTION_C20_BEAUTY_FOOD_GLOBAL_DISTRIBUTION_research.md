## 1. Selection rationale
C20 is still shown as a low-coverage Priority 0 archetype in the static No-Repeat Index, while the conversation-local ledger has already built a C20 30-row floor. This pass therefore behaves as a Priority 1 cleanup toward the 50-row operating band rather than another first-pass floor fill. The key residual risk is not that K-food/K-beauty distribution works; it is that apparel, retail, department-store, and generic consumer-brand rows can be accidentally absorbed into C20 when the true mechanism belongs to C18 or C19.

Mechanism analogy: C20 should be a cold-chain export shelf that shows sell-through, reorder, OPM/revision, and working-capital discipline. Apparel retail rows are a neighboring shelf with a similar consumer label, but different inventory physics. Mixing the shelves makes the scanner think every consumer rebound is food/beauty global distribution.
