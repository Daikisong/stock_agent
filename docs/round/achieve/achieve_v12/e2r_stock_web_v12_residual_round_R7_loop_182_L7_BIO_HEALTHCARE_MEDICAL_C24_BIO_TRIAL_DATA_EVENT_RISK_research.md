## 13. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

Current profile correctly demands non-price evidence, but C24 needs a sharper distinction among three things: clinical-data strength, regulatory-review cleanliness, and partner/commercial value capture. HLB resubmission and CRL rows show that Stage2 evidence can be too generous when the process restart has not cleared CMC/review risk. Alteogen rows show the opposite residual: partner-confirmed platform rights and Phase 3 non-inferiority can be missed or treated too late if the model waits for final approval instead of recognizing commercial option value.

## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison

No Stage3-Green row is proposed. C24 Green remains inappropriate unless approval/commercial launch or royalty recognition is visible. Stage2-Actionable is valid only for partner-confirmed or data-confirmed rows with remaining review risk explicitly attached. Stage3-Yellow is allowed for Merck/Alteogen when Phase 3 non-inferiority plus FDA/launch schedule provide a clearer bridge.

## 15. 4B Local vs Full-window Timing Audit

HLB resubmission and Alteogen Daiichi license are not price-only rows, but both show event-cap/high-MAE behavior. The appropriate output is local 4B or staged-entry guard, not full Green. HLB first and second CRLs require 4C on pre-event exposure but not automatic short-after-crash treatment because relief rallies appeared after the close.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit

- HLB first CRL: hard 4C should protect pre-CRL exposure; after-crash close needs false-4C audit.
- HLB second CRL: true unresolved regulatory thesis break, but post-crash rebound path means 4C timing must be tied to pre-event exposure.
- Alteogen rows: no 4C; drawdowns are valuation/event-cap risk rather than thesis break.

## 17. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate

`L7_BIO_EVENT_DATA_VALUE_CAPTURE_AND_REGULATORY_OVERHANG_GATE_V2`

L7 bio event rows should require the event to be sorted into one of four boxes: data-only, regulatory-process, partner-commercialization, or rejection/CRL. Data-only and resubmission-only rows remain Stage2-Watch unless they contain value-capture evidence. Partner-commercialization rows can promote earlier. CRL rows route to 4C only for exposure that existed before the CRL or when the deficiency is unresolved and future value capture is blocked.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate

`C24_TRIAL_DATA_REGULATORY_REVIEW_AND_PLATFORM_VALUE_CAPTURE_GATE_V2`

C24 should not score "good clinical data" and "regulatory approval path" as the same bridge. A stronger canonical gate is:

1. Clinical efficacy/safety data: supports Stage2-Watch or Stage2-Actionable.
2. Regulatory cleanliness: needed before Stage3-Yellow/Green; CRL/CMC uncertainty imposes 4B/4C overlay.
3. Partner quality and commercialization rights: can raise Stage2/Yellow earlier when milestone/royalty/supply path is explicit.
4. Event-cap/valuation: late partner or resubmission headlines with high MAE stay under staged-entry guard.
