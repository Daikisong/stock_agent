## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison
The R13 finding is not “Stage2 too early” in general. Stage2 is early in the way a passport gate is early: it works when the document is real. The problem is fake passports: label/proxy evidence that looks like a bridge but does not contain order, customer, margin, renewal, reimbursement, or commercialization conversion. Green should remain strict; Stage2 should require a verified bridge family or stay at watch.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit
This loop is primarily Stage2 false-positive review, so hard 4C is secondary. The false-positive rows do not all deserve immediate 4C; most should first become local 4B/watch until issuer-level thesis evidence breaks. Only rows with explicit commercial, contract, trust, or regulatory break should route to hard 4C.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate
`R13_stage2_false_positive_bridge_filter`: Stage2/Stage2-Actionable may remain eligible only if at least one verified bridge family exists: direct order/backlog, customer capacity lock, repeat channel reorder, partner/license economics, verified renewal/retention, reimbursement/procedure volume, or capital return bridge. Label-only, source-proxy-only, or relative-strength-only rows should remain watch/local 4B until bridge evidence arrives.
