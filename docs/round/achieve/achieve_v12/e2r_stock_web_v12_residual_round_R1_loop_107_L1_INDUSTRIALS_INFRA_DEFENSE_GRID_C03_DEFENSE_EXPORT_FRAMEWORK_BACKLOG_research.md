## Research thesis
C03 is not “defense theme went up.” It is the more exact bridge where a sovereign customer or state-backed procurement path becomes funded backlog, delivery schedule, and margin/cash conversion. The false-positive residue is easy to see in the market: a turret, satellite, naval, ammunition, or MRO label can pull price like a magnet, but without an executable order book and delivery margin, the magnet lets go and the chart snaps back.
