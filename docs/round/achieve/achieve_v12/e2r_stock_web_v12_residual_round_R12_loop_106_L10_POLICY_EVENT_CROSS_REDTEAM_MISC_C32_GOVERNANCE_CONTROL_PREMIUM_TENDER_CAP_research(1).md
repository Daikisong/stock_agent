## Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test
The current calibrated proxy already blocks most pure price-only blowoff from positive Stage3. The remaining C32 error is narrower: governance rows with early MFE are still tempting to treat as actionable because the market reacts before the legal cash path is visible. C32 needs a tighter split:

1. **Tender/cash path present**: tender offer, squeeze-out route, dividend/return policy, or legally binding minority cash mechanism exists. This can support Stage3-Yellow or Local-4B if the price cap is understood.
2. **Control transfer without minority cash**: ownership reshuffle, holdco value-up, or governance headline without a direct shareholder payout. This remains Stage2/Stage2-Actionable at most.
3. **Tender cap / failed closing**: early price MFE can be large, but full 4B is blocked until completion, ratio, price, and acceptance conditions are verified.
