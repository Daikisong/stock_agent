## 4. Residual interpretation

C21 behaves like a bank book where the asset is not the headline itself but the path from ROE durability to capital return. A value-up headline can lift the whole sector, but the row only becomes calibration-useful when the company-level bridge is visible: sustainable ROE, CET1 or capital buffer, buyback/dividend execution, and a credible payout runway. KB금융, 하나금융지주, 신한지주, and 키움증권 show the positive side of this bridge: MFE expands while MAE remains relatively controlled.

The failure mode is the look-alike: insurance and small financial names can show a low-PBR/value-up move, but their economics are often governed by CSM, reserve adequacy, rate-cycle earnings, or short-lived beta. 현대해상 and 한화손해보험 illustrate why Stage3 should be capped when the capital-return bridge is not confirmed and MAE remains large. 삼성화재 shows that 4B can be preserved only as a watch/full-4B candidate after non-price bridge verification.
