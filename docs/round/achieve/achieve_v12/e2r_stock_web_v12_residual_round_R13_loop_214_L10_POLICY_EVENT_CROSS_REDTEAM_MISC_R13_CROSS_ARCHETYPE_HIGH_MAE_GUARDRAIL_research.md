## 6. Residual diagnosis

```text
observed_failure_mode:
  - C01/C05: order, backlog, or construction recovery evidence can still suffer post-peak drawdown or working-capital stress.
  - C15: price-pass-through or spread evidence can be real but too temporary for Stage3-Green.
  - C16: direct offtake/resource supply can be Actionable, but policy/proxy/governance contamination creates large MAE and Green risk.

interpretation:
  - high_MAE_or_post_peak_drawdown is not a universal Stage2 deletion signal.
  - if a direct second bridge survives, Stage2 / Stage2-Actionable should be preserved.
  - escalation to Stage3-Yellow / Stage3-Green requires repeated cashflow, margin, working-capital, offtake, or recognized revenue conversion.
  - hard Stage4C still requires confirmed non-price thesis break plus weak offset quality.
```
