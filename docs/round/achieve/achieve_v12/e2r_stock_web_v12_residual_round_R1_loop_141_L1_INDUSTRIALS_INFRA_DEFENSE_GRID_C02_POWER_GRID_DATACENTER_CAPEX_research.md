## 6. Residual finding

C02 should not treat every AI-power-grid headline as the same rerating animal. The evidence ladder needs three rungs: `named order/customer` → `delivery or revenue-recognition timing` → `margin/FCF bridge`. When the first rung exists but the second and third are missing, the case can earn Stage2-Actionable but should carry a local 4B watch if 30D/90D/180D MAE becomes large. 세명전기 and 비츠로테크 show that hard contracts can still suffer deep interim MAE before the market sees delivery/margin proof. 보성파워텍 and 제일일렉트릭 show the opposite danger: broad AI-grid or Eaton-supply narrative can generate fast upside, but without explicit project-level revenue bridge it becomes a high-MAE false-positive trap. 피앤씨테크 is the cleaner Stage2 watch: lower drawdown, real power-grid automation product fit, but still not enough for Stage3-Yellow without fresh order conversion.
