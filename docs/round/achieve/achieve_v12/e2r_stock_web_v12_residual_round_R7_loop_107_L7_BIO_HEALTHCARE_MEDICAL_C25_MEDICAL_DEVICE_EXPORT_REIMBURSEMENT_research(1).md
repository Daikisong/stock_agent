The static No-Repeat ledger had C25 in Priority 0 at 6 rows, and the local v12 ledger has since lifted C25 through the 30-row floor. The core residual error is simple: the market often rewards the words device, medtech, dental, aesthetic, CGM, implant, or imaging before the business model proves repeat revenue. A device sold once is a spark. A consumable/reimbursement/service loop is the engine. C25 should score the engine, not the spark.
