## Core Finding
R13 accounting/trust validation behaves like the weight scale under the evidence table. A case may have contract, backlog, capacity, policy, or customer evidence, but if cash collection, delivery/revenue recognition, OPM/FCF, reserve quality, or off-take/control-premium clarity is missing, the price path often turns the same Stage2 evidence into a deep-MAE false positive. The guardrail should not delete all upside: rows such as `047040`, `045100`, `011930`, and `010130` show real MFE, but they still need local 4B or price-validation overlays after vertical rerating.
Aggregate: positive-with-guardrail `4`, counterexample/watch `12`, 4B/watch `10`, 4C `0`, avg MFE180 `26.6867`, avg MAE180 `-30.702`, rows with MAE180 <= -30% `8`.

R13_ACCOUNTING_TRUST_PRICE_VALIDATION_GATE_CASH_COLLECTION_REVENUE_MARGIN_RESERVE_OR_OFFTAKE_CLARITY
