Evidence summary: LaserSel product pages describe laser selective reflow / laser compression bonder for next-generation semiconductor packages; March 2024 Korean coverage tied laser bonding interest to AI/HBM spread.

## 7. Shadow rule candidate

```text
C09_INCREMENTAL_ORDER_CONVERSION_AND_CROWDING_GUARD
```

Candidate behavior:

1. Give C09 Stage2-Actionable credit only when advanced-equipment identity is paired with at least one fresh bridge: named customer, supply contract, PO, qualification, shipment, or revenue/margin conversion.
2. Downgrade to Stage2-watch or local 4B when the evidence is a product/technology article, market-size story, or analyst theme after a large price extension.
3. When MFE30 is already above roughly 30% and no incremental non-price evidence appears, attach a local 4B/profit-lock overlay rather than upgrading to durable Yellow/Green.
4. For unique-tool monopolies such as HPSP, keep the moat credit but subtract a crowding penalty when the evidence source itself frames the stock as already valuation-leading.
5. Allow later reopen only after both price reset and changed evidence family; do not reuse the same technology story to create a fresh Stage3.

Confidence: **medium-low**. This is a useful follow-up to loop 119, but C09 still needs additional new-symbol coverage to pass the 30-row minimum stability zone.
