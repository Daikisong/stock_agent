### C06-000660-20240320-HBM3E-MASS-PROD

SK hynix direct C06 positive. The March 2024 trigger had all three Stage2 ingredients: next-generation HBM3E production, Nvidia shipment route, and booked HBM capacity. The price path confirms that a Stage2-Actionable label would have caught most of the 90D upside while keeping 30D MAE shallow.

### C06-000660-20240502-SOLDOUT-2025

Still a positive direct C06 row, but the 180D drawdown after the July peak shows that C06 needs a profit-lock overlay after capacity lock becomes consensus. This is not a reason to weaken Stage2; it is a C06-specific 4B timing rule.

### C06-000660-20240926-12L-HBM3E

A later product-generation trigger with moderate 90D MFE and very strong 180D MFE. This row weakens the naive idea that late HBM headlines are always 4B: direct 12-layer mass production still had structural 180D upside.

### C06-005930-20240524-QUAL-FAIL

Samsung direct C06 counterexample. HBM optionality existed, but customer qualification was not passed. This row justifies a hard customer-qualification gate before Stage2-Actionable/Yellow credit.

### C06-005930-20240807-PARTIAL-PASS-NO-DEAL

Partial 8-layer HBM3E approval without signed supply and without 12-layer approval is a classic C06 false positive. The 90D and 180D MAE were large while MFE remained capped. This row is the cleanest no-deal cap evidence.

### C06-005930-20241008-AI-LAG

The profit-warning/apology row converts the earlier qualification uncertainty into a hard 4C route. It is not a sector-wide memory 4C; it is specifically a customer qualification and high-end AI memory lag 4C.

### C06-402340-20241122-HOLDCO-PROXY

SK Square is not a memory producer, so it should not receive the same direct C06 score. But the 20% SK hynix stake, market value discount, and buyback/cancellation route created a separate value-realization bridge. This row adds a C06 holding-proxy exception: allow Stage2-Actionable only when NAV realization or shareholder-return execution is attached.

## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison

Stage2-Actionable works for direct SK hynix when customer/capacity lock is explicit. Stage3-Yellow should require at least one of:

```text
- named customer qualification or shipment route
- capacity sold out/booked into next year
- product-generation leadership with shipment timing
- holding-company proxy plus shareholder-return/NAV realization bridge
```

Green remains intentionally strict. None of these rows justify lowering the global Green threshold. The C06 fix is not a global threshold change; it is a qualification/capacity bridge gate.

## 15. 4B Local vs Full-window Timing Audit

C06 4B is not a single sell signal. Three forms appear here:

```text
1. consensus capacity lock after rapid SK hynix reprice -> local profit-lock overlay
2. partial qualification/no deal -> 4B cap on promotion
3. holding-company NAV proxy after fast reprice -> volatility overlay, not thesis break
```

The SK hynix May row had +43.15% MFE180 but also a -41.77% drawdown from peak. That argues for local 4B after reprice, not for deleting the positive C06 signal. The Samsung August row had only +7.36% MFE180 and -33.20% MAE180, so the 4B/no-deal cap should have blocked upgrade.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit

Hard 4C should be reserved for customer qualification failure/delay that damages the HBM thesis, not for every HBM volatility patch.

```text
Samsung May 2024: thesis_break_watch_to_4C, because Nvidia qualification failure was reported.
Samsung Oct 2024: hard_4c_success, because profit warning and AI chip delay confirmed business impact.
SK hynix May/Sept 2024: not 4C; drawdown/profit-lock only.
SK Square Nov 2024: not 4C; holding discount volatility only.
```
