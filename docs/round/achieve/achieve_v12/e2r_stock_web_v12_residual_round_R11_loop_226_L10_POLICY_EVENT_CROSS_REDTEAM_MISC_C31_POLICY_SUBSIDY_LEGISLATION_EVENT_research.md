## 13. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test
The current profile tends to credit policy events once non-price evidence exists. That is directionally right for C31, but this loop shows policy/tariff evidence must be split into five gates: public program, issuer route, pass-through, cash conversion, and price phase.

- current_profile_false_positive: KOGAS, Samchully-style tariff headline if promoted to Yellow/Green without receivables/cash evidence.
- current_profile_too_early: high-MFE but high-MAE regional gas suppliers where event premium required local 4B-watch.
- current_profile_correct: low-beta route-only suppliers that should remain Stage2-watch.

## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison
Stage2 is justified by the KOGAS public event and regional supplier route. Yellow requires an issuer-level pass-through or cashflow bridge. Green is not supported by this slice alone because even the direct KOGAS event still carried receivables/debt and phase risk.

## 15. 4B Local vs Full-window Timing Audit
High-MAE event names (`036460`, `053050`, `117580`, `071320`) should not be hard 4C because the issuer route survived. They are 4B-watch: the policy evidence remains true, but the price path becomes event-premium heavy.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit
No row receives hard 4C. There is no direct cancellation, legal block, or route death. The lesson is not thesis death; it is cashflow finality and sizing.

## 17. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate
L10 policy/event rows should split public policy existence, issuer route, legal/tariff finality, issuer-level cashflow sufficiency, and price phase. A government-approved tariff opens the gate; it does not prove the listed issuer walked through with cash.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate
C31 needs a tariff-to-cashflow ladder: policy headline -> issuer route -> tariff/product pass-through -> receivables/margin bridge -> cash conversion -> phase sanity. Policy headline alone remains Stage2-watch; Actionable requires issuer route; Yellow requires direct margin/receivables bridge; Green waits for cash conversion. High-MAE policy-premium rows become local 4B-watch, not hard 4C.
