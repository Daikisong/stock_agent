## 7. Current calibrated profile stress test
C07 should reward actual HBM equipment orders and customer qualification only when the chain reaches `named order → delivery / revenue recognition → margin bridge`. The residual error here is that the same HBM word can mean three different things: a signed order, a development/qualification headline, or a final-test optionality event. The first can unlock Stage3-Yellow; the second should stay Stage2 with a Green cap; the third often needs a local 4B watch because price can peak before conversion evidence arrives.

- Correct positive: `042700` where SK hynix named order + HBM capacity route produced high MFE with very low initial MAE.
- Positive but 4B-sensitive: `031980` where Micron/DRAM3 supply evidence was real, but the peak came quickly and 90D/180D MAE became severe.
- False positive / premature Stage3: `095340`, `053610`, `420770`, where development, smaller named order, or final-test evidence lacked confirmed delivery/revenue/margin conversion.
