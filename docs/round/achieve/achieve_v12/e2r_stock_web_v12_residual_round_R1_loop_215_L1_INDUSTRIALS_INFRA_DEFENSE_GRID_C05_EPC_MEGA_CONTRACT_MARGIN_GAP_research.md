## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison

Stage2-Actionable works best where the event is a direct issuer route: HJSC's patrol-vessel contract and HD Hyundai Mipo's turnaround bridge. Stage3-Yellow is safer for positive-but-not-yet-cash rows. Stage3-Green should remain available for HD Hyundai Heavy when margin conversion and price path both align, but it should be capped for Samsung Heavy and HD KSOE result-only rows when high-MAE phase risk is visible.

## 15. 4B Local vs Full-window Timing Audit

Rows with high early MAE after a result/order headline are marked as local 4B-watch candidates, not full 4B or hard 4C. This matters most for HJSC's record-order headline, Hanwha Ocean's Q1 turnaround, Samsung Heavy's late Q2 result, and HD KSOE's consolidated result row.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit

No trigger in this loop is a clean hard 4C. High MAE is a smoke alarm, not the fire. Hard 4C would require repeated cost overrun, order cancellation, funding/customer route death, accounting trust break, or working-capital damage that breaks the thesis evidence itself.

## 17. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate

L1 EPC/order/result evidence should stay capped unless it bridges to cost-rate control, working-capital release, and cash-conversion freshness. Contract backlog is the pipe; margin and cash are the water moving through it.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate

C05 should use a result-quality and price-phase ladder: direct award/order route -> realized margin or cost-rate proof -> working-capital/cash conversion -> persistence and price-phase sanity. Record-order or result-only rows after rerating should cap below Green and attach local 4B-watch. High MAE with a surviving issuer route is not hard 4C.

## 19. Before / After Backtest Comparison

Baseline/current: average 90D MFE and 180D MFE remain strong, but false-positive Green risk appears in the record-order/result-only rows. The proposed shadow guard does not lower global thresholds; it changes C05 evidence attribution so that margin/cash and phase sanity decide Green.
