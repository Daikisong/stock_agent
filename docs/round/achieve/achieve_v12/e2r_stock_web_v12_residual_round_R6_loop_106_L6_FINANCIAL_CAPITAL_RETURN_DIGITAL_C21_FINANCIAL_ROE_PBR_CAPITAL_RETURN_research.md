## 4. Interpretation

C21 should behave like a bank ledger, not like a headline tape. A PBR discount is only the opening balance. The usable rerating comes when ROE durability, capital buffer, payout/buyback execution, and balance-sheet quality reconcile into shareholder cash return. The positive rows are mostly large banks and brokerages where price path and non-price bridge can align. The counterexamples are small financials or insurance-dominant rows where the value-up label has insufficient company-level execution bridge or belongs more cleanly to C22.

This loop strengthens the distinction between three mechanisms: bank/brokerage ROE-PBR capital-return execution, insurance CSM/reserve/rate-cycle reroute, and value-up label price-only beta. The current calibrated profile can still mistake the third for the first unless C21-specific bridge and reroute gates are applied.
