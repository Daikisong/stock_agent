## 3. Trust Diagnosis

A price row is a footprint, not the animal. In v12, the footprint must be tied back to the creature that made it: revenue recognition, backlog conversion, margin, FCF, payout, reimbursement, renewal, installed-base service, or minority cash. The row may show MFE, but if it is source-proxy-only and its accounting bridge is unverified, it should not be allowed to become Green or full 4B.

This R13 pass separates three states:

1. **Accounting-trust positive pending**: plausible but still requires profile/shard and evidence URL repair.
2. **Mixed accounting trust**: there is a mechanism, but cross-canonical contamination or MAE requires caps.
3. **Blocked by accounting / price validation**: deep MAE, price row reuse, or missing cash bridge blocks promotion.
