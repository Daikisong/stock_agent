## 3. Core finding

C05 should not treat a backlog, order, PF-relief, or guidance headline as a straight Stage3 unlock. The bridge must pass through four gates:

```text
signed/backlog/guidance evidence
  -> revenue recognition timing
  -> realized margin / cost-ratio repair
  -> working-capital, receivable, PF, or cash-collection clarity
  -> only then Stage3-Yellow/Green eligibility
```

This holdout set shows both sides. HL D&I, Hyundai E&C, Daewoo E&C, KCC E&C, and DL E&C all produced meaningful MFE when some form of margin repair, big-bath reset, PF conversion, or guidance bridge appeared. 계룡건설 is the counterweight: existing-order cost pressure was visible, so backlog without realized margin bridge should not be promoted. Even in the positive rows, post-peak drawdowns of `-18%` to `-36%` argue for local 4B watch after fast repricing.
