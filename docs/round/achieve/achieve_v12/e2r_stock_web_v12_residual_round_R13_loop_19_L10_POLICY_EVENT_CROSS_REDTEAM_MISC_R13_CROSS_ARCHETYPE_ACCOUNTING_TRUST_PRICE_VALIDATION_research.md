## 3. Mechanism Diagnosis

R13 accounting trust is a lock on the vault door. A row may have price MFE, and the story may sound right, but if the cash bridge has not been verified, the vault does not open. The bridge can differ by sector:

- finance: payout, buyback, ROE persistence, reserve quality;
- industrials: order-to-revenue, project margin, working capital, FCF;
- battery/materials: utilization, AMPC capture, customer call-off, spread-to-margin;
- healthcare: endpoint validation, reimbursement, royalty, commercial cash conversion;
- platform/content/software: ARR, retention, segment OPM, recurring monetization;
- governance: minority cash path and tender cap.

The central residual error is reused-price-row overconfidence. A reused row is useful as a map pin, but not as a passport. Before it can enter production scoring, it needs profile/shard refetch, corporate-action window check, and source evidence repair.
