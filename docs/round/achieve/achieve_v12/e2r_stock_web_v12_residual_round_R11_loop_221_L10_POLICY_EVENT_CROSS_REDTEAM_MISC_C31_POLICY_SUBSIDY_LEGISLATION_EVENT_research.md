## 13. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

- P0 current profile risks over-crediting `policy_or_regulatory_score` when the source is official but issuer-level conversion is absent.
- Stage2 bonus is acceptable for clean positives (`166090`, `092870`) but too generous for proxy rows with deep 90D/180D MAE.
- Yellow/Green should require cashflow finality; broad policy package should not cross the Green threshold by itself.
- Local 4B watch is needed for `089790` and other policy-after-spike rows.
- Hard 4C is not proposed because policy route death was not directly proven.

## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison

No Stage3-Green representative row is emitted. This is intentional: broad legislation/support-package rows are not enough for Green unless they connect to issuer-level order, subsidy receipt, recognized tax credit, margin bridge, or cash conversion.

## 15. 4B Local vs Full-window Timing Audit

The 2024 package rows with early local peak and severe subsequent MAE show that C31 needs 4B-watch when policy headlines arrive after a rerating. The guard should be non-price aware: the source is official, but the missing bridge is issuer cashflow.

## 16. 4C Protection Audit

No hard 4C trigger is proposed. The event type is broad policy support; failure is over-promotion from headline to issuer cashflow, not confirmed policy cancellation or listed-company thesis death.

## 17. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate

L10 policy/event evidence should split public program existence, ecosystem coverage, named issuer route, funding/tax-credit finality, cashflow sufficiency, and price phase. Official policy validates that the gate opened; C31 promotion requires evidence that the issuer can pass through the gate and keep the cash.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate

C31 should add a policy-support proxy ladder: law/package headline -> ecosystem eligibility -> named issuer route -> recognized funding/tax-credit/order -> margin/cash conversion. Semiconductor ecosystem support without issuer conversion remains Stage2-watch; policy after local spike becomes 4B-watch; Green requires cashflow finality.

## 19. Before / After Backtest Comparison

The P2/P3 shadow profiles reduce proxy false positives by discounting broad policy support unless direct issuer conversion is present. They keep the two clean positives as Stage2/Actionable rather than rejecting the policy theme entirely.
