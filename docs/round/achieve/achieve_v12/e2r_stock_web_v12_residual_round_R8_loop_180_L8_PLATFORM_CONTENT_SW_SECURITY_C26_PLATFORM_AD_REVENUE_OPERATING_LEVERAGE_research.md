## 4. Case Notes
### 4.1 NAVER — C26 works only when search/ad/commerce growth becomes operating-quality evidence
NAVER provides the cleanest within-company timing test. The Q4 2023 and Q1 2024 entries both had legitimate platform-growth vocabulary, but they were not clean C26 Actionable rows: event-close entries produced `MAE_180D=-27.36%` and `-22.43%`, respectively. The machine should treat those as Stage2-Watch because the bridge was real but the timing and competitive/cost context were not yet favorable.

The inflection became clean in Q2 and Q3 2024. Q2 had record OP and a search-platform growth bridge from commercials, while Q3 showed record operating profit and double-digit search-platform reacceleration. Their paths were the opposite of price-only hope: Q2 produced `MFE_180D=46.91% / MAE_180D=-4.87%`; Q3 produced `MFE_180D=67.61% / MAE_180D=-2.56%`. Q4 2024 remained fundamentally strong, but `MAE_90D=-22.55%` says event-close buying still needed staged-entry risk control.

### 4.2 Kakao — platform growth can be a false friend when cost, trust, and non-operating drag dominate
Kakao Q3 2023 is a useful trapdoor. It had platform monthly-user and TalkBiz growth, and the stock rallied from a depressed base, but operating profit fell and infrastructure/subsidiary costs plus SM-related trust overhang remained. This row must not teach the model that weak operating leverage is good merely because `MFE_90D=35.60%` followed a prior derating.

Kakao Q4 2023 is the classic false positive. Operating profit and platform sales rebounded, but net loss, impairment, cost, and trust drag overwhelmed the headline. The stock path was `MFE_180D=2.74% / MAE_180D=-43.66%`. C26 therefore needs a clean net/operating-quality bridge, not just a revenue or OP-growth number.

## 5. Proposed C26 Shadow Rule Candidate
```text
rule_id = C26_OPERATING_QUALITY_TIMING_AND_COST_DRAG_GATE_V2
positive_gate = search/ad/commerce or user-inventory growth + operating-profit quality + low/high-MAE timing check
confirmation_gate = search-platform ad efficiency, commerce monetization, OPM/revision proof, or second-quarter confirmation
block_gate = platform revenue growth if OP declines, costs/trust/non-operating losses dominate, or event-close entry has MAE90 <= -20% without follow-up confirmation
stage_behavior = early Stage2-Watch for legitimate growth vocabulary; Stage2-Actionable only after operating-quality bridge; Stage3-Yellow only when price path and evidence both confirm; local peak entries get 4B/high-MAE guard
```

Mechanically, C26 should act less like a loudspeaker and more like a cash register. A loudspeaker repeats the word “advertising”; a cash register asks whether traffic became revenue, revenue became margin, and margin survived costs, trust issues, and entry timing.
