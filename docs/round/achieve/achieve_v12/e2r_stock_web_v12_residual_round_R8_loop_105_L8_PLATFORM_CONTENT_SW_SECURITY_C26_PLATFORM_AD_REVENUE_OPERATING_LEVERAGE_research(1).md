## 17. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate

`L8_PLATFORM_CONTENT_SW_SECURITY` should continue to distinguish verified platform operating leverage from label-only relative strength. This is sector-specific, but the stronger version is better scoped to C26 because C27 and C28 have different bridge mechanisms.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate

Proposed canonical rule: `C26_verified_platform_ad_payment_revenue_margin_bridge_required_before_Yellow_or_Green_plus_label_spike_to_local_4B_watch_v2`.

Mechanism: platform traffic, ad recovery, commerce GMV, or payment volume should not be enough by itself. The bridge has to land in revenue, operating leverage, or durable margin evidence. Without that bridge, the same label should be treated as local 4B watch, not positive rerating.
