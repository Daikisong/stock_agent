## 6. Residual findings

### Sector-specific rule candidate

L1 EPC / plant-service / ship-equipment rows should split service or product-route evidence, direct customer/order finality, realized margin bridge, working-capital/cash conversion, and price phase. A route or product page is only the pipe; margin and cash are the water moving through it.

### Canonical-archetype rule candidate

C05 should use a plant-service / ship-equipment order-to-margin-cash guard:

```text
route evidence -> direct customer/order finality -> margin/cost proof -> working-capital/cash conversion -> phase sanity
```

Route evidence can justify Stage2/Actionable, and direct customer orders can justify Stage3-Yellow. Stage3-Green should wait for margin/cash freshness. High-MAE rows with a surviving service/product/customer route should become 4B-watch, not automatic hard 4C.
