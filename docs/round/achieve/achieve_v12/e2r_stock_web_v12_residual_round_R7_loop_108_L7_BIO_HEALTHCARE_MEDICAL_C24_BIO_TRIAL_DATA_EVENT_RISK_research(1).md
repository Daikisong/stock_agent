## 3. Residual Mechanism

C24 is the archetype where a single data readout can change probability mass. It is also where false positives are most seductive: a pipeline keyword, regulatory rumor, or trial-resume headline can look like a catalyst before endpoint quality and reviewer risk are known.

The boundary is narrow:

- **C24 proper**: binary trial data, endpoint quality, regulatory probability update, failed/redo trial risk.
- **C23 contaminant**: approval-to-commercialization, royalty, reimbursement, revenue bridge after approval.
- **C25 contaminant**: medical device export, installed base, reimbursement, consumable/service repeat revenue.

A C24 row can be Stage2 early, but Stage3-Green requires endpoint/regulatory evidence that survives more than the first price spike. Deep MAE after a trial spike is not noise; it is often the event risk revealing itself.
