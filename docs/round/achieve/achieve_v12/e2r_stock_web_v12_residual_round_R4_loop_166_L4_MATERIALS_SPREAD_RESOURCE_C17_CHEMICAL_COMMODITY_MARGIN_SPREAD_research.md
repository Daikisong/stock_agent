## 6. Residual diagnosis

C17 needs a **spread durability + feedstock cost + valuation reset** gate. The same word, “spread,” behaves differently depending on where the company sits in the commodity chain:

1. **NCC/basic chemicals:** loss narrowing is not enough. Stage2-Actionable requires sustained product spread, utilization, and segment margin bridge. Lotte Chemical 2024-05-10 is the trap; Lotte Chemical 2025-02-07 is the reset-audit exception.
2. **Synthetic rubber/spandex:** specialty-like commodity exposures can rerate before quarterly earnings if supply digestion, demand recovery, and feedstock-cost relief are visible. Kumho 2025 and Hyosung TNC 2024-01-30 are the useful positives.
3. **Chlor-alkali/ECH:** group-level earnings beat is not enough when the commodity segment remains loss-making. Lotte Fine Chemical 2024-04-30 and 2024-10-31 prevent false Stage2.
4. **Refining:** inventory effects and oil-price gains should not be treated as recurring margin quality. S-Oil 2024-04-26 blocks headline-only Stage2; S-Oil 2025-01-24 allows staged recovery after reset.
