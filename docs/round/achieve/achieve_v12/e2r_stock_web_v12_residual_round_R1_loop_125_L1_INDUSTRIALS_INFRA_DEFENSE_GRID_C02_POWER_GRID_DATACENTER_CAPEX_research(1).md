## 1. Selection rationale

The remote No-Repeat Index still places `C02_POWER_GRID_DATACENTER_CAPEX` at the lowest Priority 0 bucket. Earlier local C02 loops already covered direct transformer, cable, switchgear, AMI, capacitor, and busduct paths, so this follow-up deliberately moves into a boundary strip: grid-service/DR/VPP platforms, SCADA/remote-control grid automation, ultracapacitor power-stabilization components, power-plant O&M, small transformer components, and a blocked subsea-cable installer.

The research question is narrow: **when does a power-adjacent label deserve C02 credit, and when is it only an IPO/product/component proxy?**
A C02 thesis should behave like a transformer connected to a live substation: it needs a named grid/datacenter customer, order/backlog, CAPA/delivery, ASP, and revenue or margin conversion. If the wire is not actually connected, the model should not let the label carry current.

## 4. Evidence split and residual interpretation

### 4.1 Gridwiz / 453450 — IPO peak vs post-washout reopen

Gridwiz is a true grid-service boundary case, not a simple product proxy. Its DR business sits between power-market dispatch and customer-side load reduction, while EM/ESS/PV/VPP plans give it a distributed-energy route. But the first tradable day was an IPO blowoff: entry close 49,500, same-day high 82,200, and then a 180D low of 13,640. That is a textbook `Stage4B` local peak, not fresh C02 Yellow.

The same symbol becomes useful only after the IPO premium burns off. The 2025-03-14 row shows 180D MFE 66.02% with a still-meaningful MAE of -22.16%. This supports a **discounted DR/VPP grid-service Stage2-Actionable route after valuation reset**, not a direct transformer/cable backlog route.

### 4.2 Vitzrosys / 054220 — SCADA/grid automation boundary

Vitzrosys contributes a smaller but clean boundary row. SCADA, remote monitoring, and distributed control are grid-automation infrastructure, but not automatically datacenter CAPEX. The 2024-04-01 entry delivered 180D MFE 22.73% and MAE -16.60%; this is enough for Stage2/watch credit but not enough for Actionable/Yellow without named utility order, contract margin, or recurring service revenue.

### 4.3 LS Materials / 417200 — ultracapacitor component proxy

LS Materials' CellDule and UltraGrid vocabulary touches AI datacenter and power-stabilization narratives. The trap is that component optionality can sound like C02 but still lack named customer/order/revenue conversion. On the 2024-05-14 entry, 30D MFE reached 23.12%, but 180D MAE widened to -63.01%. That path says: tradable theme, not durable C02 rerating.

### 4.4 Enertork / 019990 — power/nuclear actuator local-4B trap

Enertork has a real power-plant component route through electric actuators and nuclear actuator qualification. Still, this is not a grid/datacenter CAPEX order. Its 2024-05-14 row had a 72.80% MFE inside 30 trading days and then a -35.52% 180D MAE. This is exactly why C02 needs a component/proxy penalty and a local 4B profit-lock after fast reprice.

### 4.5 KEPCO Industrial / 130660 — O&M contract is not grid CAPEX backlog

KEPCO Industrial has real power-sector contracts, but O&M and fuel-environment maintenance contracts are not the same as transformer, cable, substation, or datacenter power equipment backlog. The 2025-03-14 row gave 90D/180D MFE 53.34%, yet the -21.30% MAE shows a volatile proxy path. C02 should allow only watch/4B-capped credit unless the contract directly expands grid/datacenter power delivery capacity.

### 4.6 Atum / 355690 — small transformer product identity is not C02 without demand bridge

Atum is a transformer company, but the relevant as-of evidence was mainly small/flat transformer and EV charger component optionality. The 2024-10-15 entry produced only 5.82% 180D MFE and -44.99% MAE. It should remain a product-identity counterexample until customer CAPEX, named demand, margin recovery, or backlog appears.

### 4.7 LS Marine Solution / 060370 — direct infrastructure route, blocked by raw share-count contamination

Subsea cable construction is closer to C02 than many component proxies. The price path itself was very strong, but the raw-unadjusted stock-web window showed a share-count change inside D+180. This row is kept as narrative-only support, not as a representative trigger.
