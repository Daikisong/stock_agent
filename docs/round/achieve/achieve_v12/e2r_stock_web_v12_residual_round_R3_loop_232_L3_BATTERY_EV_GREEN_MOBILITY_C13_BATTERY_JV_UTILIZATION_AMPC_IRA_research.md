## 7. Residual interpretation

C13 is not one evidence type. It is a ladder. A product route, a JV headline, a customer qualification, a conditional order, a booked order, utilization, ex-credit profit, and cash conversion are different rungs. A route can be real and still fail as an entry if it arrives after rerating or if the demand/utilization pipe is dry.

Failure modes isolated here:

1. **Route noun overpromotion** — TAPEX, Zaigle, Enix, Phil Energy, and NanoTeam show that product/JV/customer-route nouns are not cashflow.
2. **Finality gap** — IPO, JV, sample, and equipment-recognition evidence must be discounted until final funding, customer calloff, order conversion, or shipment/utilization appears.
3. **Reset-route positives** — Lake Materials, Kangwon Energy, and MPlus show that route evidence after reset can deserve Stage2-Actionable or Yellow, but Green still waits for utilization/cash.
4. **High-MAE survival guard** — high MAE alone is not hard 4C when the customer/product route survives; it is a 4B/watch or sizing problem unless non-price thesis death is confirmed.
