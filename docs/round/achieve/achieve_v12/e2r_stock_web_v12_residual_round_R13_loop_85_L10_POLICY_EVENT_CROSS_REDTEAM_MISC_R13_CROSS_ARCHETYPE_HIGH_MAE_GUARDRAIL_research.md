## 4. Research question

Across loop85, the same error shape repeated under different names:

```text
nuclear policy keyword
HBM / semiconductor beta
battery orderbook headline
copper/resource policy theme
beauty reopening/channel spike
digital-finance beta
approval/commercialization keyword
digital ad theme
mobility/OEM/EV-parts theme
construction/PF relief theme
late grid-CAPEX extension
value-up policy theme
```

Each can look plausible on the first candle. But if the bridge is missing, the move is a painted doorway: it looks like an entrance until price tries to walk through it.

R13 therefore tests this cross-archetype guard:

```text
If bridge_missing_or_proxy_only
AND ((MFE90 < 10 AND MAE90 <= -20) OR (MFE90 < 15 AND MAE180 <= -30))
THEN block Stage2-Actionable/Yellow
AND route to Watch / 4B-risk / evidence-quality repair.
```

Positive-control exception:

```text
Do not block when the original canonical bridge is present and MFE90 >= 20 with tolerable MAE.
```
