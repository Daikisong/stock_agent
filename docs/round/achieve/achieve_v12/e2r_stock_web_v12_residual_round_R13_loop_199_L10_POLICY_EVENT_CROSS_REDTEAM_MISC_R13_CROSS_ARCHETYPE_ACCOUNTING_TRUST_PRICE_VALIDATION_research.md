## 4. Research question

Accounting trust is a different metal than earnings growth. A company can show a huge forward price path because a control-premium war ignites the share price; another can show a deep drawdown because an engineering or audit failure has broken the license to trust the numbers. This R13 file separates those two mechanisms so source-sector weights do not drink from a contaminated river.

The holdout asks three questions:

1. When does a non-price accounting or operating-permission break deserve hard 4C even before the full price damage is known?
2. When should a spectacular price path be routed away from C16/C05 operational evidence because governance/control-premium contamination dominates?
3. Which bad accounting headlines should remain Stage4B/watch because explicit offset quality survives?

## 6. Case notes

### 6.1 HDC Hyundai Development — accounting/operating-permission break as hard 4C

The HDC Hyundai Development rows provide clean hard-4C controls. The source evidence family is not a price-only drawdown; it is a construction-quality and operating-permission trust break. The 2022-01-12 and 2022-01-17 rows both show low 180D MFE and deep 180D MAE, while the 2022-03-30 business-license suspension row confirms that the thesis had moved from margin gap into permission/trust break.

Residual label: hard 4C should trigger when the non-price failure attacks the company's right to execute projects, not merely when the stock already sold off.

### 6.2 C16 strategic-resource rows — operational evidence versus price contamination

POSCO Holdings, LS Corp, and LX International retain Stage2 or Stage2-Actionable credit only when the evidence includes a company-specific lithium, EV-battery-material, or nickel route. Korea Zinc is the opposite control: C16 can see enormous 180D MFE, but the price path is governance/tender-contaminated. Those rows must be routed to C32/R13 contamination logic, not used as C16 operational Green proof.

Residual label: a price path can be real and still be the wrong evidence. It is like a thermometer placed next to a furnace: the heat exists, but it does not measure the room.

### 6.3 Kumyang narrative-only row — hard-4C taxonomy without forward-window usability

Kumyang is the qualitative shape of an accounting-trust hard 4C: audit disclaimer, trading suspension, and resource-theme failure. But the stock-web forward window is insufficient after the event row. Therefore it remains narrative-only and cannot be used in aggregate weight calibration.
