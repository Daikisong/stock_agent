## 14. Stage2 / Yellow / Green Comparison

- Stage2 and Stage2-Actionable can begin at issuer-level route or direct order evidence.
- Stage3-Yellow requires margin/cost-rate or backlog visibility.
- Stage3-Green remains capped unless working-capital/cash conversion is present.
- Engineering-rank or source-profile rows without margin/cash bridge are capped at Stage2-watch or local 4B-watch.
