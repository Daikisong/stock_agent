## 5. Case Synthesis

C02 is not a generic electricity-theme bucket. It behaves like a substation: power only becomes useful when the switchgear, cable, transformer, capacitor, and delivery schedule are all connected. `010120`, `000500`, and `009470` show the positive route: backlog, ASP, capacity, and component demand aligned before the large price move. Their 90D MFE was large while MAE stayed manageable enough to justify Stage2-Actionable or Yellow.

The counterexamples show the opposite route. `001820`, `001440`, and `017510` still produced headline MFE, but the drawdown pipe burst afterward. In these cases the price label moved faster than entity-level backlog-to-margin proof. Under the current calibrated profile, the residual error is not that the global Stage2 bridge gate is wrong; the error is that C02 needs a **canonical-specific bridge-family test** before theme beta can graduate from Watch into Yellow/Green.
