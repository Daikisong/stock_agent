## 7. Residual Diagnosis
C10 failure is no longer a lack of semiconductor-cycle evidence. The remaining failure is that the same broad wind is being treated as issuer-level water.

- `425420`, `311320`, `049080`, `090470`, and `089790` show that route evidence can look like a bridge but still fail when order, shipment, utilization, or margin/cash conversion is absent.
- `254120`, `217190`, and `083500` show why direct product-route evidence should not be discarded: the initial MFE was real, but the later 180D drawdowns argue against automatic Green.
- The ladder should keep the first useful route evidence as Stage2/Actionable after reset, but route evidence after a local rerating should become 4B-watch unless conversion follows.

## 8. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate
`L2_AI_SEMICONDUCTOR_ELECTRONICS` rows should split memory-cycle weather, issuer-specific product/tool/material route, customer qualification, booked order or shipment conversion, utilization/margin bridge, and price phase. The memory upcycle is wind; only issuer-level orders, shipments, utilization, and margin/cash turn the turbine.

## 9. Canonical Archetype Rule Candidate
`C10_MEMORY_RECOVERY_EQUIPMENT_CYCLE` should use a route-to-conversion ladder:

```text
generic memory/HBM/CXL recovery
  -> issuer product/tool/material route
  -> customer qualification
  -> booked order or shipment
  -> utilization / margin / cash conversion
  -> price-phase sanity
```

Route evidence can be Stage2-Actionable after reset. Direct order or result bridge can support Yellow. Green waits for conversion. Generic proxy after rerating becomes local 4B-watch. High MAE with a surviving customer/product route is not hard 4C by itself.
