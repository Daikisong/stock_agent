## 6. Residual diagnosis

C16 needs a **policy/supply/commercial-conversion** gate. The words “critical mineral,” “lithium,” “nickel sulfate,” “copper,” “AI grid,” and “supply-chain sovereignty” are not equivalent.

1. **Plan vs plant vs offtake vs run-rate:** POSCO 2024-01-22 is a long-range plan and fails as immediate actionable entry. POSCO 2024-11-29 adds plant + named offtake. POSCO 2025-02-14 adds a valuation-reset audit after battery-materials losses, while the plant/offtake bridge remains alive and the forward path works better.
2. **Strategic technology vs governance premium:** Korea Zinc 2025-01-17 has a real national strategic technology bridge, but C16 should not count all subsequent price movement as nickel-sulfate rerating when governance/tender premium is also present.
3. **Macro resource proxy vs company conversion:** I-Gu and Daechang have copper narrative exposure, but proxy-only articles should not unlock Stage2-Actionable. Poongsan has a more explicit conversion bridge: fabricated copper volume/price and defense revenue/order visibility.
4. **4B exit discipline:** Some resource rows deliver large MFE quickly, then reverse sharply. C16 needs a local 4B exit guard whenever MFE is driven by commodity-price oxygen rather than realized company-level margin.
