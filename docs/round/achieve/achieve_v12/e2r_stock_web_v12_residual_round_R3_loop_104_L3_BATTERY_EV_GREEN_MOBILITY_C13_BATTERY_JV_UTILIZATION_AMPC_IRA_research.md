## 8. Residual Interpretation
C13 is not a battery-theme bucket. It is a conversion test. Policy subsidy, AMPC, or JV language is only valuable when it becomes company-level cash: production starts, utilization rises, customer call-off appears, ASP/mix or margin improves, and cash conversion survives working-capital drag. Without that bridge, the stock can still flash a bright 30D/90D MFE, but the path often behaves like a sparkler: hot early, then high 180D MAE.

The sector-specific residual is therefore a split gate. Direct cell/JV economics and verified component qualification can receive Stage2-Actionable or Stage3-Green watch. Indirect battery material labels, parent-holdco exposure, and separator/resource narratives should be capped at Stage2 or local 4B watch unless customer utilization and cash capture become visible.
