Mechanism: insurance stocks can look deceptively similar to C21 value-up banks when the market is rewarding low PBR and capital return. But the gearbox is different: bank rerating transmits through ROE, payout, buyback, and credit-cost visibility; insurance rerating has to pass through CSM durability, reserve adequacy, solvency capital buffer, underwriting/rate cycle, and payout execution. Without that bridge, the price move is a shiny dashboard light, not proof that the engine is turning.
