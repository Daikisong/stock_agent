## 4. Residual diagnosis

C30 is a trapdoor archetype. It looks like one thing, but three mechanisms are hiding under the same floorboard.

First is **PF/liquidity break**: failed refinancing, debt-workout filing, PF guarantees, self-performed project stress, or a broken financing chain. This is a hard 4C candidate, but only clean price windows should enter aggregate calibration.

Second is **construction-quality trust break**: fatal or repeated accidents, rebuild obligations, regulatory suspension, brand/license impairment, and cancellation risk. GS E&C and HDC show that this is not a generic sector downcycle. The evidence attacks the company’s ability to convert backlog into trusted earnings.

Third is **ordinary builder beta with a balance-sheet bridge**: DL E&C, Hyundai E&C, and Daewoo E&C show that weak housing sentiment can coexist with cash, cost-rate discipline, order selectivity, and later recovery. The model should avoid permanent 4C scars after cleanup. But it should also refuse Yellow/Green when the only bridge is backlog, low PBR, or government liquidity support.

The residual error is a **decay/reopen problem**. Hard 4C should be fast when PF/liquidity/trust break is explicit. It should not be immortal. After cost-base reset, regulatory clarity, or profit normalization, the same symbol can reopen into Stage2-Actionable, while still carrying a 4B/profit-lock overlay if the price move becomes front-loaded.

## 5. Proposed shadow rule candidate

```text
canonical_archetype_rule_candidate:
C30_PF_TRUST_BREAK_LIQUIDITY_GATE_WITH_DECAY_REOPEN_AFTER_CLEANUP

rule_body:
- If a builder has confirmed PF liquidity stress, debt-workout application, high PF guarantees, failed refinancing, or restructuring evidence, route to Stage4C unless the price window is blocked by corporate action/restructuring contamination.
- If a builder has a fatal/repeated quality accident, rebuild obligation, regulatory suspension, license impairment, or brand/orderbook trust break, route to hard 4C even if backlog remains large.
- If a builder has cash/net-cash, refinancing capacity, cost-rate discipline, order selectivity, cost-to-complete visibility, or profit normalization after cleanup, allow Stage2-Actionable.
- Do not promote to Stage3-Yellow/Green from backlog, low PBR, government liquidity support, or construction beta alone.
- A prior 4C can decay/reopen only after executed cleanup, cost-base reset, settlement/regulatory clarity, or profit normalization.
- Add local 4B/profit-lock overlay when 30D/90D captures most of the 180D upside or post-peak drawdown exceeds roughly 30%.
```
