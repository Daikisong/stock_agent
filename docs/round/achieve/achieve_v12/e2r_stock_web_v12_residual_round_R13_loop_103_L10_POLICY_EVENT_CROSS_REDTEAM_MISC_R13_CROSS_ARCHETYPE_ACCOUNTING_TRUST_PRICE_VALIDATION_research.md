## 4. Cross-Archetype Accounting / Trust Findings

### 4.1 Hard trust break is not the same as ordinary 4B valuation risk

GS건설 and HDC현대산업개발 show that full rebuild, construction-quality collapse, and brand/legal cost shock should route through a hard trust-break gate. The forward paths are not identical: GS건설 later rebounded, while HDC had near-zero forward MFE and deep MAE. The rule should not say “trust break means no rebound ever.” It should say: **until rebuild cost, legal process, liability scale, and execution credibility are revalidated, Stage3 is blocked and hard 4C/RedWatch remains the default.**

### 4.2 PF/liability and EPC margin gaps should block contract-value overpromotion

금호건설, 삼성E&A, 현대건설, 아바코, and 원익피앤이 show a common trap: large contract, backlog, or customer-name evidence can open Stage2, but if the accounting surface contains PF liability, lump-sum cost exposure, delivery timing, revenue recognition, or working-capital uncertainty, Stage3 should wait. The price path repeatedly shows high MAE or low MFE when the system treats gross contract value as durable rerating.

### 4.3 Operating loss, impairment, inventory adjustment, and commercialization gap are price-validation gates

한온시스템, 삼성SDI, 이스트소프트, 원익IPS, and 네패스 show the second trap: revenue growth, technology route, or demand recovery language can sound constructive while accounting/operating evidence remains weak. If loss, impairment, inventory adjustment, utilization break, or commercialization delay is visible, the Stage2 evidence must be discounted until the margin/revenue conversion clears.

## 6. Proposed Shadow Rule Candidate

```text
R13_ACCOUNTING_TRUST_PRICE_VALIDATION_GATE_REBUILD_PF_LOSS_MARGIN_DELIVERY
```

Rule sketch:

```text
IF non-price evidence includes one of:
  - construction quality / rebuild / brand-trust break
  - PF, contingent-liability, liquidity, or debt-scale stress
  - operating loss, impairment, inventory adjustment, or utilization break
  - margin/working-capital gap on a lump-sum or mega EPC contract
  - customer contract with delivery/revenue-recognition timing risk
  - technology/commercialization route without customer revenue bridge
THEN block Stage3-Yellow/Green until accounting/revenue/margin conversion evidence is visible.
IF realized path also shows low MFE and high MAE, route to Stage4B/RedWatch or Stage4C depending on whether the evidence is a soft bridge gap or a confirmed thesis break.
```
