## 1. Selection Rationale

This loop returns to C18 after loop 177's apparel-OEM pass and after the later R13 holdouts. The raw No-Repeat Index still shows C18 as a Priority 2 quality-repair zone, so the goal is not raw row inflation. The goal is a different C18 sub-leaf: **K-food export channel reorder**, where export growth, mainstream retail penetration, overseas subsidiary sales, or supply expansion can look like an E2R bridge, but only becomes calibration-useful when it reaches listed-entity revenue, margin, and repeat-channel conversion.

This file avoids the previous C18 apparel-OEM set and uses K-food / packaged-food exporters: 삼양식품, 농심, CJ제일제당, 대상, 풀무원, 롯데칠성, and 매일유업. It also avoids treating C20's broad K-beauty/K-food global-distribution rule as sufficient; C18 here is narrower: **export channel evidence must behave like reorder**, not merely brand popularity.

## 6. Residual Findings

### 6.1 Export-channel growth must be separated from late rerating geometry

삼양식품 is the clean C18 control. The May 2024 trigger had direct export-channel conversion: Buldak overseas sales and operating-profit surprise were already visible in the listed entity. The June 2024 trigger still had the correct thesis, but the price path changed character: 180D MFE was still strong, while 90D MAE became deep enough to require staged-entry or local 4B guard. The conveyor belt was real; the entry point was no longer clean.

### 6.2 K-food popularity is not the same as listed-entity money road

CJ제일제당 and 매일유업 show the bridge gap. CJ제일제당 had global-food language, but the food operating-profit bridge was not strong enough for the listed-entity path. 매일유업 had a specific China supply expansion channel, but it was too narrow to move consolidated economics. C18 should not promote single-channel export news unless it can carry the listed entity.

### 6.3 Reset-positive cases should not be hard 4C

농심 and 롯데칠성 show why food cost/marketing drag should not automatically become hard 4C. The negative FY2024 headline was real, but after the reset the forward MFE/MAE geometry improved sharply. The correct state is Stage4B-Watch / false-hard-4C audit, not irreversible thesis break.

### 6.4 Moderate positives need scale confirmation

대상 and 풀무원 are not clean Green unlocks. 대상 has a real K-food export capacity story through Jongga/O'Food, US production, and Poland expansion, but the stock-web path is moderate. 풀무원 had delayed positive behavior: early MAE was deep, but 180D MFE reopened. Both are Stage2-Watch or staged-entry examples, not automatic Stage2-Actionable.
