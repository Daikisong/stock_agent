## 3. Core finding

C12 should not treat a customer-contract, equipment-order, supply-order, or contract-expectation headline as completed rerating evidence. The required bridge is:

```text
customer contract / order / PO expectation
→ delivery timing
→ revenue recognition / shipment repetition
→ ASP or margin bridge
→ call-off clearance / customer ramp visibility
```

If the bridge is missing, Stage2 may remain open, but Stage3 persistence should be capped or routed to local Stage4B watch.
