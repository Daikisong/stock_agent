### Specific answers required by v12
1. Current calibrated profile is directionally correct in requiring a Stage2 bridge, but C05 remains too permissive when a signed mega EPC award is treated as margin visibility by itself.
2. Actual MFE/MAE says C05 is not a pure backlog archetype. Hyundai E&C, Samsung E&A, and DL E&C had real evidence but weak 180D return-to-risk because entry happened before cost, margin, or working-capital confirmation.
3. Stage2-Actionable can stay available for named Aramco/Shaheen/Fadhili/Amiral-style awards, but Stage3-Yellow should require at least one of: confirmed revenue-recognition schedule, improving cost ratio, revision upgrade, or FCF/working-capital conversion.
4. 4B logic should be local and full-window separated. Entry-day or early peak behavior in 000720 and 375500 should cap positive staging immediately; later full-window peak drawdown in 006360 and 047040 should not erase the positive case, but should add watch risk.
5. No hard 4C rule is proposed from this loop alone because no row shows contract cancellation or an irreversible EPC thesis break. The loop proposes a 4B/watch cap and a Stage3 margin bridge gate.
