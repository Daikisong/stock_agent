## 3. Residual Mechanism

The positive rows share three properties: funded sovereign customer, order conversion, and margin/delivery visibility. Counterexamples lack one of those gears. Component names, space-policy labels, and tension beta may create early MFE, but without funded backlog they should remain Stage2 or Local-4B-Watch.
