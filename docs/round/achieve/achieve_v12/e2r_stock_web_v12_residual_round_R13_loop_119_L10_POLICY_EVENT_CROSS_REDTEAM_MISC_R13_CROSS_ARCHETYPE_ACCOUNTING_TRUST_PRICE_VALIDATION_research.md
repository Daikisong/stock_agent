## 4. Residual finding

The recurring residual is that **valid evidence does not automatically mean validated accounting, trust, cash, or execution bridge**. Contracts, orders, policies, long-duration supply agreements, reserve/CSM headlines, PF liquidity events, spread recovery, or approval/commercialization narratives can all be real. The R13 question is whether the row has enough cash collection, revenue recognition, margin, FCF, reserve quality, shipment, call-off clearance, offtake clarity, or execution proof to survive a risky price corridor.

This run does not hard-route these rows to Stage4C. Without explicit thesis break, the correct action is usually Stage2-Watch or `Stage4B-LocalWatch` overlay until the second bridge is confirmed.
