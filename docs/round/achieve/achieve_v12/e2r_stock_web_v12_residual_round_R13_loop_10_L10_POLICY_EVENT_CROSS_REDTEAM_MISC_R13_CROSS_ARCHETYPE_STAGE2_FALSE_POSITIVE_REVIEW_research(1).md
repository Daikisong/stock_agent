## 1. Selection rationale
The v12 runner is coverage-index-first, but the conversation-local ledger has now pushed most C01~C32 sector archetypes through local 30-row floors and many through local 50-row operating bands. The most valuable next step is not another sector floor-fill. It is a cross-archetype R13 checkpoint that asks whether Stage2 / Stage2-Actionable rows are being overtrusted when the price path shows deep 90D/180D MAE and the evidence is still source-proxy only.

This loop selects `R13_CROSS_ARCHETYPE_STAGE2_FALSE_POSITIVE_REVIEW`. It focuses on rows where Stage2 was useful as an early watch layer, but where the same row must not be promoted into Stage3-Green or full 4B until company-level non-price bridge, stock-web profile/shard refetch, corporate-action window verification, and original evidence URL repair are complete.
