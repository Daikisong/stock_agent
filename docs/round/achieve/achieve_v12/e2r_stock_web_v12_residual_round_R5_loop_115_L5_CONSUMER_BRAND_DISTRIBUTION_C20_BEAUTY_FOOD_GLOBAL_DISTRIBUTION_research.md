## 4. Interpretation
C20 is not simply “food/beauty stock went up.” The useful bridge is like a distributor shelf that keeps refilling: initial sell-in must become sell-through, reorder, OPM/revision, and cash conversion. If only the label exists, the price path often has a fast local peak and then behaves like a returned shipment: MFE appears first, but MAE carries the truth later.
- Positive: 004370 shows that a repeated food export/reorder route can deserve Stage2-Actionable treatment when margin and channel evidence are visible.
- Mixed positives: 001680 and 003960 support a C20 bridge, but the later drawdowns say the rule must include inventory/AR and post-peak high-MAE guards.
- Counterexamples: 007310 and 005610 show that global food/brand labels without sell-through or OPM/revision bridge should remain Stage2-Watch or local 4B.
