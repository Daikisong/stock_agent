## 3. Guardrail Question

The profile already has two global safety rails: price-only blowoff blocks positive stage, and hard 4C thesis break routes to 4C. The residual error is that **4B and 4C can still be confused**.

```text
4B = event cap, entry heat, bridge exhaustion, late headline, or local blowoff. It brakes promotion but does not declare the thesis dead.
4C = issuer-level economics, trust, order, court/vote, call-off, or accounting axis is irreversibly broken.
false-hard-4C = weak headline after valuation reset where forward MFE reopens and no irreversible non-price break exists.
control positive = hard bridge and price path align; generic red-team guard must not suppress it.
```

## 5. Findings

### 5.1 Local 4B is the common failure mode, not hard 4C

HD현대마린솔루션, 대양전기공업, 삼양식품 late headline, 농심 first export-channel row, CJ제일제당, and 풀무원 all show why a hot entry should usually become local 4B or no-promotion rather than hard 4C. The bridge may be incomplete or late, but the thesis is not necessarily dead.

코오롱글로벌 and 동원개발 reset rows are the key C30 audit. They look like construction/PF weakness at the headline layer, yet forward MFE reopens after valuation reset. The correct treatment is Stage4C-Watch / false-break audit until a second non-price break confirms hard 4C.

### 5.3 Hard-bridge controls keep the guardrail honest

동성화인텍 2025, 세진중공업, HJ중공업, 계룡건설, 삼양식품 early, 농심 reset, and 동원개발 reset prove that R13 must not become a universal pessimism switch. If issuer-level money road and conversion bridge are visible, 4B/4C red-team should preserve the signal and only manage entry geometry.
