## 2. Why C04 needs a Priority1 cleanup

C04 is not simply “nuclear theme up.” The mechanism is narrower: policy headline → legal/project gate → final contract or project scope → company-level revenue/margin/FCF. When that pipe is open, the price path can graduate beyond Stage2. When it is missing, the same headline behaves like steam trapped in a sealed valve: the pressure rises quickly, then vents through deep MAE.

This cleanup separates four subfamilies:

1. `service_revenue_bridge_positive`: O&M or plant service revenue has a visible cash path.
2. `final_contract_scope_bridge`: project scope or engineering work is visible but not yet fully translated into margin.
3. `component_label_false_positive`: instrumentation, valve, control, or inspection exposure without order conversion.
4. `post_headline_local4B`: large MFE after policy headline, but too much MAE for Green/full 4B.
