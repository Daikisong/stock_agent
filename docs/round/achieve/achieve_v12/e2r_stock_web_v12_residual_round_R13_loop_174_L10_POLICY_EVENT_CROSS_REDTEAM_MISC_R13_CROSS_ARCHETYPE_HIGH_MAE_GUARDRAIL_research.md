## 5. R13 finding

High-MAE errors were not uniform. The rows split into three different mechanisms:

1. **No-MFE / deep-MAE false positives.** These should usually be blocked or demoted because the market never paid for the evidence. Examples: 엔켐, ISC late report, 기가비스, 심텍, TCC스틸, 삼성전자 2024-07, 엘앤에프 2024-03.
2. **High-MAE structural successes.** These should not be hard-blocked because the thesis later produced large 180D MFE, but they need staged entry, confirmation, or position-size guard. Examples: 산일전기, 테크윙 late Samsung-order, Lunit.
3. **Low-MAE controls.** These are early enough or bridge-rich enough that a high-MAE guard should not over-block them. Examples: 와이씨 2024-04 early order and 삼성전자 2023-11 memory recovery.

### Stage evidence separation

- **Stage2 evidence that survived:** named order/backlog/customer bridge in 산일전기, 테크윙, 와이씨 early-order control, and broad early memory recovery in 삼성전자 2023-11.
- **Stage2 evidence that failed:** contract/guidance/product-vocabulary without call-off, qualification, margin, or run-rate conversion in TCC스틸, 삼성전자 2024-07, 심텍, 엘앤에프.
- **Stage4B evidence:** MAE30 <= -20% with MFE90 capped below +10% or late-entry after prior relative-strength extension.
- **Stage4C evidence:** non-price thesis damage after previous hype, especially confirmed operating loss or loss-conversion after pilot/order vocabulary.
- **R13 high-MAE distinction:** the guard is not a blanket block. If 180D MFE is strong after a deep interim drawdown, the correct action is staged-entry / smaller sizing / delayed confirmation, not hard rejection.

### Proposed gate logic

```text
if MAE_30D_pct <= -20 and MFE_90D_pct < 10 and hard_conversion_bridge_missing:
    downgrade positive Stage2/Stage2-Actionable to Stage4B-Watch or Stage2-Watch

if MAE_90D_pct <= -35 and MFE_90D_pct < 15:
    require fresh non-price bridge before any Stage2-Actionable or Stage3-Yellow promotion

if MAE_90D_pct <= -35 and MFE_180D_pct >= 35 and hard_bridge_confirmed:
    do not hard-block; attach staged-entry, sizing, and 4B-exit guard

if high_MAE is caused only by volatility but MFE path is already strong and evidence bridge is hard:
    keep Stage2 but block Green until drawdown/peak-decay stabilizes

if MAE_90D_pct <= -40 and MFE_180D_pct < 15:
    classify as residual false positive unless 4C evidence is already explicit
```

The main correction is that **MAE is a path variable, not a thesis variable**. A deep drawdown can mean "wrong thesis", "right thesis too early", or "right thesis entered after oxygen was gone." The profile should therefore react to MAE through the MFE/MAE asymmetry and evidence bridge, not through a single hard cutoff.
