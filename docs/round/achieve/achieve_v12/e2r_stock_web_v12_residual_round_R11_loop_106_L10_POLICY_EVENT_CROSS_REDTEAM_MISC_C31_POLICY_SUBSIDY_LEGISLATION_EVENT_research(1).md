## 2. Selection rationale

`V12_Research_No_Repeat_Index.md` still shows C31 as a low static coverage area. Conversation-local work has already reached the C31 30-row floor, so this pass changes the purpose from minimum coverage to **Priority 1 static-to-50 cleanup**.

C31 is not meant to swallow all financial value-up, insurance, or utility rows. It is a policy wrapper. The row belongs in C31 only when the policy, law, budget, tariff, subsidy, or official program is the causal engine that turns into company-level cash flow. If the dominant mechanism is ROE/PBR/payout, it should route to C21. If the dominant mechanism is CSM/reserve/rate-cycle quality, it should route to C22.

## 7. Score-return alignment / current profile stress test

C31's main residual error is not that policy events are ignored. The error is that a broad policy label can masquerade as a company-specific Stage3 story before cash capture is visible. The policy headline is like a gate opening at the top of a canal: water may enter the system, but the question is whether it reaches this company's turbine.

Stress-test summary:

```text
C31_positive_when:
  direct policy / law / budget / tariff / subsidy mechanism exists
  and company-level revenue, margin, capital return, or FCF bridge is visible
  and 90D/180D MAE guard is tolerable after stock-web refetch

C31_stage2_cap_when:
  policy headline exists
  but the company-level cash bridge is indirect, parent-contaminated, or sector-beta-only

C31_reroute_to_C21_when:
  ROE/PBR/value-up/payout/buyback execution is the actual mechanism

C31_reroute_to_C22_when:
  insurance CSM/reserve/rate-cycle/solvency is the actual mechanism

C31_block_until_reverify_when:
  source_proxy_only == true
  or evidence_url_pending == true
  or stock-web profile/shard refetch has not been completed
```
