The tested mechanism is: **customer contract / equipment order headline → shipment timing → revenue recognition → margin/cash conversion**. C12 should not promote every supply-contract headline into Stage3. The positive side requires either named customer quality plus delivery visibility or later revenue conversion. The counterexample side occurs when large order headlines create a Stage2 bridge, but call-off, delivery timing, project execution, customer confidentiality, or margin recognition is too thin to support Stage3.
