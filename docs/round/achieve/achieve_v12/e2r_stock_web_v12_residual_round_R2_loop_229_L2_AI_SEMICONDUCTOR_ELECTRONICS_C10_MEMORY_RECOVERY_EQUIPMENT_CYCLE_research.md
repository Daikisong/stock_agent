## 7. Residual Diagnosis
The common failure is not that C10 needs more cycle-weather evidence. The problem is that the same wind is being treated as water moving through every issuer's pipe.

- `080220`, `036810`, `033640`, `097800`, and `171010` show that product-route or industry-cycle evidence can look like a bridge but still collapse when order, shipment, utilization, or margin/cash conversion is absent.
- `089970` and `059090` show why direct order or sharply issuer-specific HBM component route should not be discarded; they can be Stage2-Actionable, but later drawdown argues against auto-Green.
- `200470` is the useful late-recovery row: early MAE was large, but result evidence plus later OSAT recovery produced 180D upside. It should be Actionable/Yellow after reset, not price-only Green at the wrong phase.

## 8. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate
`L2_AI_SEMICONDUCTOR_ELECTRONICS` rows should split memory-cycle weather, issuer-specific product/material route, customer qualification, booked order or shipment conversion, utilization/margin bridge, and price phase. The memory upcycle is wind; only issuer-level orders, shipments, utilization, and margin/cash turn the turbine.

## 9. Canonical Archetype Rule Candidate
`C10_MEMORY_RECOVERY_EQUIPMENT_CYCLE` should use a proxy-to-conversion ladder:

```text
generic memory/HBM/CXL/LPDDR recovery
  -> product/material route
  -> customer qualification
  -> booked order or shipment
  -> utilization / margin / cash conversion
  -> price-phase sanity
```

Route evidence can be Stage2-Actionable after a reset. Direct order or result bridge can support Yellow. Green waits for conversion. Generic proxy after rerating becomes local 4B-watch. High MAE with surviving customer/product route is not hard 4C by itself.
