## 24. Score-Price Alignment Verdict

R6 supports a two-lane calibration rule. For bank/insurance/financial-holdco capital-return cases, early Stage2A is the engine room: once value-up/capital-return evidence and price relative strength ignite together, waiting for full Green pushes entry toward a hotter part of the curve. For digital finance IPOs, the same price strength is a mirage unless earnings bridge, ROE path, and valuation sanity are visible.
