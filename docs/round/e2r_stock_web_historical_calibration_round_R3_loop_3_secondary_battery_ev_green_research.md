## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit
1. Stage2/Stage2-Actionable entries were strongest in Enchem, Daejoo, and Chunbo. Enchem was extreme, so it simultaneously validates early promotion and 4B overheat handling.
2. SKIET is the counterexample: early MFE existed, but the evidence was event premium/IPO valuation rather than durable EPS/FCF. The selected profile rejects it.
3. Old gate problem: in the successful R3 material cases, waiting for Green was like arriving at the charging station after the battery had already overheated. The current needed to be tapped earlier, but with a temperature sensor attached.
