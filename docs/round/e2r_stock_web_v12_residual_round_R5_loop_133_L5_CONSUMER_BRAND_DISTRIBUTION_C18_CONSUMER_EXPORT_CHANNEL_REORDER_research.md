## 2. Core Finding
C18 should behave like a customs gate, not a billboard. A brand can wave an overseas flag through licenses, stores, and articles, but E2R should only stamp Stage2-Actionable or Yellow when country-level revenue, repeat reorder/sell-through, and margin conversion are visible. Otherwise the model confuses export optionality with export machinery. The loop also shows a second gear: after a successful export rerating, late overseas-growth articles should often become Stage4B overlay, not another fresh Stage2.
