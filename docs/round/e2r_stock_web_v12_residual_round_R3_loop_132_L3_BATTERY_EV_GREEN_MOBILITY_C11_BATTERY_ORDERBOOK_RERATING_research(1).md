## 5. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

### 5.1 Positive/control side

PNT shows the clean C11 path: an equipment orderbook was not just a slogan; it was already moving toward revenue recognition. Its 90D MFE exceeded 100%, but the later drawdown-after-peak also says the correct treatment is **Stage2-Actionable + local 4B/profit-lock**, not a permanent Green.

Samsung SDI and LOTTE Energy Materials show a different path. They are not classic equipment backlog cases. The signal is **production/supply or customer inventory normalization after a price reset**. C11 should allow limited Stage2-Actionable credit here only when the customer-volume route is already visible.

### 5.2 Counterexample side

LGES, SK Innovation, Solus, and L&F are the guardrail side. They all contain legitimate battery-orderbook or supply-chain words, but the price paths show that **contract size, cell-maker identity, battery-foil identity, or long-term customer optionality** is not enough. Without shipment/call-off survival, utilization, OP margin, or FCF conversion, the current profile still risks false positives.

## 6. Shadow Rule Candidate

```text
canonical_archetype_rule_candidate = C11_ORDERBOOK_REQUIRES_DELIVERY_CALLOFF_UTILIZATION_AND_MARGIN_BRIDGE_WITH_CONTRACT_HEADLINE_4B_CAP
```

Mechanism:

```text
IF battery_orderbook_or_supply_contract_headline
AND NOT delivery/revenue-recognition/call-off/utilization/margin-bridge visible
THEN cap at Stage2-watch or local Stage4B.

IF orderbook is already converting into revenue recognition, production, customer shipment, or post-reset inventory normalization
THEN allow Stage2-Actionable.

IF 30D/90D MFE is already large and non-price evidence has not upgraded from orderbook to margin/FCF
THEN attach local 4B/profit-lock overlay.
```
