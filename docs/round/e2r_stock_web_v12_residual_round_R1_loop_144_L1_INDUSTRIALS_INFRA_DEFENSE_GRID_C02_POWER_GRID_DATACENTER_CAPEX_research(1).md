## 3. Residual hypothesis
C02 should not promote every power-label or data-center-adjacent name. The useful split is:

- `Stage2-Actionable`: power grid / datacenter power / facility infra proxy plus actual order, backlog, capacity, shipment, or margin bridge.
- `Stage3-Yellow`: only after bridge evidence persists beyond the first price expansion and the 4B guard is not active.
- `Stage4B watch`: label-driven price expansion where order, margin, or FCF conversion is missing or already fading.

This loop is deliberately source-proxy-heavy. It is useful for residual shape and MFE/MAE calibration, but promotion should remain blocked until URL repair replaces proxy-only evidence.

## 5. Interpretation
The four positive paths show that C02 can extend beyond transformer/cable pure plays into electrical control, facility utility, and power EPC names, but only with bridge evidence. The three counterexamples show the opposite: power-supply or facility labels alone can create a fast price echo and then a deep MAE, so C02 needs a local 4B watch when the bridge is missing.

The mechanism is like a substation busbar. It is not enough that a cable is nearby; current must actually flow through the bus. In scoring terms, the current is backlog/order/revenue/margin conversion. Without it, the ticker is only wearing the C02 uniform.
