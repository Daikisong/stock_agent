## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit

- **SK하이닉스:** Stage2/Stage2-Actionable captured the HBM rerating before Green. Green was thesis-valid but late and carried much worse 90D MAE.
- **HLB:** Stage2 produced high MFE, but binary approval risk blocks clean Green. Correct label is capped Actionable/event premium until final approval evidence closes.
- **GS건설:** ordinary Stage2/Green evidence failed because safety/accounting trust risk dominated. The after-profile should produce no entry rather than a weaker Green.
- **씨젠:** policy/disaster evidence was already actionable; full Green was profitable but late. 4B later worked as full-window valuation/positioning overlay.
- **팜스토리:** Stage2/Yellow event premium worked; late Green without durable margin/revision bridge had high MAE and should be demoted.

## 24. Score-Price Alignment Verdict

R13 verdict: **early evidence should be promoted, but only through a RedTeam overlay.** When the market has already moved and company-specific EPS/OP/FCF bridge is present or policy/disaster demand is concrete, Stage2-Actionable / Yellow often beats Green.
