## 1. Round Scope

R12 loop 5 validates agriculture, food-security, education-service, and food-service/life-service archetypes. The loop deliberately mixes two durable service rerating cases, two agriculture/event-premium cases, and one failed online-education event case. The practical question is whether Stage2 can be promoted earlier without allowing every event spike to become Stage3-Green.

The selected shadow profile is `r12_stage2_actionable_service_plus_event_guard`: service names need revision/margin bridge; agriculture shock names may become `Stage2-Actionable-event-only`; event-only education spikes need a hard MAE guardrail.

## 6. Evidence Source Map

Evidence is recorded as historical public evidence descriptors, separated from the Stock-Web price rows. The two agriculture/feed cases use global food-supply shock evidence after Russia's full-scale invasion of Ukraine and the March 2022 FAO food-price shock. Service cases use public earnings/revision/reopening descriptors; those descriptors are not used alone for weight changes unless the OHLC backtest and component breakdown support them. COVID/online education uses public school-closure/remote-learning event evidence and is treated as a counterexample because price risk was too high.

External context sources used for narrative mapping: FAO/Axios record food-price shock after Russia-Ukraine war; Reuters/official public COVID school-closure coverage; Korea-focused salt/food-panic event references were not used for selected rows in this loop.
