## 1. Round Scope

R12 tests agriculture, food-security, education, food-service, and other life-service archetypes. The core question is whether early Stage2 evidence can become an actionable tier without letting event-only spikes become false Green entries.

## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit

Stage2 worked best in the two durable service cases because MAE was shallow and the evidence path had a margin/revision bridge. In the feed/grain cases, Stage2 also produced extreme MFE, but the same entries had deep MAE and were driven by a macro event premium rather than a verified company-specific earnings bridge. 아이스크림에듀 is the key counterexample: event-driven remote-learning MFE was high, but drawdown and MAE were too severe for Stage2-Actionable without a strict risk filter.

## 13. Stage3 Yellow / Green Lateness Audit

Green lateness was material in service cases. 메가스터디교육 Green lateness ratio is about 0.49 and CJ프레시웨이 is about 0.92, meaning the Green trigger was close to the late part of the move. For event-premium agriculture/feed cases, Green lateness alone is not the answer: even if Green is earlier, without margin/revision evidence it becomes a false-positive risk.

## 14. 4B Timing Audit

The 4B audit splits local peak from full-window peak as required. 팜스토리 and 미래생명자원 had useful price-only 4B watches near the event blowoff. However, because the 4B evidence type is `price_only|positioning_overheat`, these should not become full 4B thesis exits. 아이스크림에듀 shows why: price-only 4B was visible, but the later drawdown was caused by failed event durability rather than a clean non-price 4B thesis break.

## 15. 4C Protection Audit

Hard 4C is not fully validated in this round. 팜스토리 and 아이스크림에듀 have thesis-break-watch rows, but the evidence is mainly fading event premium and price damage. The round therefore proposes no hard 4C weight delta.

## 16. Baseline Score Simulation

Baseline_current_proxy tends to select later Green or late Yellow rows in this R12 sample. That protects against some early event noise but misses service rerating entries. In feed/grain and online-education event cases, baseline Green can become false positive when it treats price momentum as proof of durable rerating.
