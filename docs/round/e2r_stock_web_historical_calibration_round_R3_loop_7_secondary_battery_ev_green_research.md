## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit
1. POSCO Future M, L&F and Chunbo show the same mechanism: early evidence was not full Green, but the market had already started repricing future EPS/OP optionality. Waiting for Green was like checking the battery temperature only after the casing was already hot.
2. SKIET is the counterexample. Early MFE came from IPO/event premium, not durable EPS/FCF. That means Stage2 promotion needs an event-premium filter.
3. Samsung SDI is the JV headline guardrail. A real factory/JV announcement without EV-demand, utilization and margin bridge can still fail badly.
4. Therefore R3 should not promote all battery Stage2 rows. It should promote early orderbook/capacity/customer-quality evidence only when event-premium, customer concentration, EV-demand, utilization, margin and valuation guardrails pass.
