## 12. Stage2 → Stage4 Audit

### 테크윙

Stage2 and Stage2-Actionable were both superior to Green. The reason is not just price momentum: the HBM test-handler use case was specific enough, and relative strength confirmed that the market was beginning to price equipment leverage before formal EPS revision was fully reflected.

```text
old_gate_problem = Stage3_gate_too_late_for_HBM_equipment
new_shadow_rule = promote_to_Stage2_Actionable_when_technology_node_relevance + relative_strength + equipment_specificity are present
```

### HPSP

This is the guardrail. Stage2 had some MFE, but MAE was severe. Yellow/Green came too close to the peak. This should not become a broad early-evidence promotion rule.

```text
old_gate_problem = Green_confirmation_can_be_false_positive_when_valuation_blowoff_dominates
new_shadow_rule = block_Green_if_positioning_overheat + weak_revision_bridge + MAE_risk are present
```

### ISC

Stage2 was valid but volatile. The local 4B row was not a full-window 4B because it was mostly price-only and later full-cycle context remained open. The correct treatment is **small-size Stage2-Actionable / Yellow watch**, not clean Green and not full exit.

### 주성엔지니어링
