## 5. Case Findings

### Positive / structural-success side

- **T01 Doosan Enerbility / 2025-01-17 settlement**: legal overhang removal converted C04 from policy/project optionality to executable project route. MFE_90D was +99.77% with MAE_90D only -8.23%. The current calibrated profile would likely wait for too much final-contract proof; that misses the legal-overhang-cleared reopen.
- **T02 KEPCO E&C / 2025-01-17 settlement**: the path was rougher, with MAE_90D -22.79%, but MFE_180D +88.68%. This supports staged entry rather than immediate Green.
- **T03 BHI / 2025-01-17 settlement**: BOP/supplier route worked when the legal overhang was removed and the company had real nuclear BOP relevance. This is not generic supplier-proxy credit; it is a supplier-spillover exception when capacity/role evidence exists.
- **T04 KEPCO KPS / 2024-07-18 preferred-bidder event**: lower MFE but much lower drawdown than small proxy names. Service/maintenance route deserves Stage2 recognition, but not full project Green.

### Counterexample / guardrail side

- **T05 KEPCO E&C / 2024-07-18 preferred-bidder spike**: peak_date was entry day. MFE_180D stayed +19.63%, but MAE_180D reached -39.94%. Preferred-bidder headline without final contract/legal clearance is a 4B/profit-lock situation after a same-day rerating.
- **T06 Woojin / 2024-07-18 proxy**: nuclear instrumentation relevance did not prevent MAE_180D -39.46%. Named project/order bridge was absent.
- **T07 Iljin Power / 2024-07-18 proxy**: nuclear maintenance/business relevance did not become a Czech project revenue bridge. MAE_180D was -42.40%.
- **T08 Doosan Enerbility / 2024-08-28 appeals**: an appeal/legal delay headline should not be hard 4C by itself. MFE_180D was +135.12% after entry. This is the key C04-specific weakening of the global hard_4c route: procedural appeal risk is not thesis break.


Rule body:

1. **Preferred bidder / policy headline**: Stage2-watch only unless a named company role, contract path, or delivery/revenue bridge is present.
2. **Same-day project-headline spike**: if `peak_date == entry_date` or local peak proximity is high and legal/final-contract clearance is missing, cap to Stage4B/watch.
3. **Legal appeal / arbitration risk**: route to 4B/legal-watch, not hard 4C, unless final contract is blocked, bidder status is lost, or the technology/license thesis is definitively broken.
4. **Legal-overhang settlement**: can reopen Stage2-Actionable even before final contract, especially for direct equipment/engineering roles or supplier names with real nuclear BOP/service bridge.
5. **Supplier proxy**: cap small-cap nuclear-related names to Stage2-watch/4B unless there is named order, named consortium role, delivery schedule, or margin/FCF bridge.
