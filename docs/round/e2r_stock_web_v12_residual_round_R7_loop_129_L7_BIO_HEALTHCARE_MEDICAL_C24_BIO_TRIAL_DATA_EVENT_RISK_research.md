## 5. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

### 5.1 Positive data cases

Yuhan and Oscotec show why C24 cannot be treated as a pure binary event. The Phase 3 MARIPOSA data had named endpoint quality, named global partner, and downstream economics. Their 180D MFE values of 73.10% and 128.27% are the clean positive-control side of this canonical. The current profile is broadly right to give Stage2-Actionable credit, but it should not jump directly to durable Green until approval, milestone, royalty, or revenue conversion is visible.

HanAll is a different species: early Phase 1 pharmacodynamic/safety data can still matter if the endpoint is mechanistically meaningful and partner-validated. But its 90D/180D path also shows the trap. It made a large MFE, then gave back heavily after the peak. The correct rule is **Stage2-Actionable first, then fast 4B/profit-lock after a data-driven vertical repricing**.

### 5.2 Failure and 4C cases

Helixmith, Kangstem, and Genexine show the strange biology of failed biotech charts. The clinical thesis is genuinely broken at the evidence layer, so hard 4C is directionally correct. But the stock path after the first crash can still show large MFE because low-price optionality, short covering, asset-sale speculation, or new pipeline hopes can re-enter the tape. That rebound is not a new positive C24 signal. It is a **4B/watch decay state** unless fresh clinical evidence appears.

### 5.3 Residual Error

The remaining C24 error is not `hard_4c_thesis_break_routes_to_4c` itself. The error is treating hard 4C as either permanently dead or immediately repaired by price rebound. The proposed C24 rule separates three states:

```text
C24_state_1_positive_data = endpoint_quality + partner_validation + economic_bridge -> Stage2-Actionable
C24_state_2_failure_event = primary endpoint/statistical failure -> hard 4C at evidence layer
C24_state_3_post_crash_rebound = price rebound without new clinical evidence -> 4B/watch, not Stage2 reopen
```

## 6. Proposed Shadow Rule Candidate

```text
canonical_archetype_rule_candidate = C24_TRIAL_DATA_REQUIRES_ENDPOINT_QUALITY_PARTNER_ECONOMICS_AND_4C_DECAY_REOPEN_GATE
rule_type = canonical_archetype_specific_exception
existing_axis_tested = hard_4c_thesis_break_routes_to_4c | stage2_actionable_evidence_bonus | price_only_blowoff_blocks_positive_stage
proposed_action = new_axis_proposed
production_scoring_changed = false
shadow_weight_only = true
```

Mechanism:

1. **Promote positive trial-data events only when endpoint quality, data maturity, partner validation, and economic bridge are visible.** A p-value or headline alone is not enough.
2. **Stage2-Actionable may be early for Phase 1/2 PD data only when the mechanism is de-risked and partner economics are real.** This is not Stage3-Green unless indication-level efficacy and commercialization path are visible.
3. **Route primary endpoint failure or statistically failed OS/PFS to hard 4C at the evidence layer.**
4. **After a hard 4C crash, price rebound alone becomes Stage4B/watch, not a positive C24 reopen.** Reopen requires new clinical design, new endpoint/subgroup evidence, new partner financing, or regulatory path repair.
