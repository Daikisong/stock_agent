## 6. Current Calibrated Profile Stress Test

The current calibrated profile already blocks many price-only blowoffs and requires non-price evidence for full 4B. C12 still needs a narrower supplier branch:

1. **Do not promote supplier orderbook labels to Yellow** unless customer acceptance, delivery, margin, FCF, or revision confirms actual pull.
2. **Do not hard-4C every weak battery supplier** when the path still shows a recovery-band MFE. Use local 4B watch unless the customer-pull break is confirmed.
3. **Do not suppress every high-MAE positive**. Daejoo shows a C12-style high-MAE positive route: early drawdown, then large MFE. The bridge should be timing-sensitive, not a blanket hard block.
