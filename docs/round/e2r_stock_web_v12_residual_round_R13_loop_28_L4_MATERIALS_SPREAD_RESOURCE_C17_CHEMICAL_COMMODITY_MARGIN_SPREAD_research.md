## 17. Sector-Specific Rule Candidate

rule_scope = `sector_specific`

Proposed L4 shadow rule: **high-MAE commodity/spread success sizing guard**. If a material/chemical spread trigger has valid product-specific evidence but the path historically shows MFE with double-digit MAE, keep promotion possible but reduce position confidence / require wider risk buffer. This protects cases like `004000` without blocking `010060`-style clean spread-duration moves.

## 18. Canonical-Archetype Rule Candidate

rule_scope = `canonical_archetype_specific`

Proposed C17 shadow rule: **product-specific spread-duration gate**. Positive C17 promotion requires at least two of: product-specific spread expansion, utilization or capacity route, financial margin bridge, and early revision signal. Generic solar/module, integrated downstream, or mixed-business valuation narratives cannot substitute for that bridge. `009830` and `011790` are the guard examples; `010060` and `004000` are the positive/guarded-positive examples.
