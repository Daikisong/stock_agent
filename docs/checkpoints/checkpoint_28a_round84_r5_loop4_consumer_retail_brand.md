# Checkpoint 28A Round 84 R5 Loop 4: Consumer / Retail / Brand

## Scope

Round 84 반영은 소비재·유통·브랜드 R5 Loop 4 케이스팩 확장이다. 핵심 원칙은 `잘 팔린다`, `미국에 입점했다`, `틱톡에서 떴다`를 구조적 Green 근거로 바로 쓰지 않는 것이다.

쉬운 예:
- `Ulta 입점`은 Stage 2 후보 신호다.
- 하지만 실제 sell-through, 재주문, OPM, 재고, 매출채권이 확인되지 않으면 Stage 3-Green 근거가 아니다.

## Guardrails

- Production scoring was not changed.
- Case records are not candidate-generation input.
- StageClassifier thresholds were not changed.
- Viral, channel entry, GMV, store count, hardware sales cannot create Green by themselves.
- Food recall, country sales ban, data breach, supplier regulation, payment delay, channel stuffing, IP litigation, product safety, tariff/import damage, discount margin break remain RedTeam fields.
- Missing stage prices and exact dates were left null instead of being invented.
